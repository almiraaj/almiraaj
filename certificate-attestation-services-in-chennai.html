What is certificate attestation and why is it important?

Certificate attestation is a crucial process that is needed for a variety of purposes, especially when it comes to the official documentation. The process of attestation involves verifying the authenticity and validity of documents by obtaining an official stamp or signature from a relevant authority. This helps to ensure that the document is legitimate, and can be accepted and recognized in a foreign country. Attestation is typically required for various types of documents, including education certificates, personal identification certificates, and civil documents.
