About Certificate Attestation in Chennai

Certificate Attestation is the process of verifying the authenticity of Indian documents for international use. It involves validation by Notary, State Departments, Embassy/Consulate, and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MOFA) of the destination country. Our Chennai-based attestation services ensure your documents are legally accepted abroad.

Why Certificate Attestation is Important?

Attestation validates your documents for employment, higher education, migration, family visas, business setup, and legal procedures abroad. Without proper attestation, foreign authorities may reject your documents, leading to delays and complications.

Step-by-Step Attestation Process

The attestation process generally includes:

  • Notary Attestation: Initial verification of the document
  • State Authentication: HRD/Home/SDM/Chamber of Commerce depending on document type
  • MEA Attestation: Ministry of External Affairs verification
  • Embassy Attestation: Legalization by the destination country‚Äôs Embassy/Consulate
  • MOFA Attestation: Final verification in the foreign country (if required)

Types of Certificates Attested

Documents commonly requiring attestation include:

  • Educational Certificates (Degree, Diploma, Mark Sheets)
  • Birth Certificates
  • Marriage Certificates
  • Power of Attorney
  • Legal Heir Certificates
  • Divorce Certificates
  • Commercial Documents (Invoices, MOA, COI, Agreements)
  • Police Clearance Certificates (PCC)
  • Affidavits
  • No Objection Certificates (NOC)
  • Other personal or business documents
