✅ Certificate Attestation in Chennai – Frequently Asked Questions

Certificate Attestation is the process of verifying Indian documents to make them valid abroad. It is required for higher education admissions, employment visas, family/dependent visas, and business setup. Learn more: Certificate Attestation Services in Chennai

Documents commonly requiring attestation include educational certificates (degree, diploma, mark sheets), personal certificates (birth, marriage, divorce, death, affidavit, power of attorney, legal heir), police clearance certificates, and commercial documents (company registration, partnership deeds, trade licenses, invoices).   5. ✅ MOFA Attestation – Final legalization abroad
