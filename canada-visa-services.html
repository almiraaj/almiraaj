Canada Visa Services

Canada is a land of opportunity, natural beauty, and cultural diversity. Whether you're planning to visit, study, work, or settle permanently, understanding the visa process is essential. Our Canada Visa Services provide expert guidance to help you navigate the requirements with ease.

Types of Canada Visas

  • Visitor Visa (Temporary Resident Visa): For tourism, family visits, or business trips (valid up to 6 months).
  • Study Permit: For international students enrolled in Canadian institutions.
  • Work Permit: For temporary employment in Canada.
  • Super Visa: For parents and grandparents of Canadian citizens or permanent residents (valid up to 5 years).
  • Permanent Resident Visas: Includes Express Entry, Provincial Nominee Program (PNP), and Family Sponsorship.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Valid passport or travel document
  • Good health and no criminal record
  • Proof of ties to home country (job, family, property)
  • Proof of sufficient funds for your stay
  • Intent to leave Canada at the end of your visit

Required Documents

  • Completed visa application form
  • Valid passport with at least one blank page
  • Recent passport-sized photographs
  • Proof of financial support (bank statements, payslips)
  • Travel itinerary and accommodation details
  • Letter of invitation (if applicable)
  • Medical exam and police clearance (if required)

Visa Fees (as of 2025)

  • Visitor Visa (per person): CAD 100
  • Visitor Visa (family of 5+): CAD 500
  • Biometrics (per person): CAD 85
  • Study Permit: CAD 150
  • Work Permit: CAD 155

Visa Duration

Most visitor visas allow a stay of up to 6 months. Super Visas allow up to 5 years. Study and work permits are valid for the duration of the program or job offer, plus a grace period.

Step-by-Step Visa Process

  1. Determine your visa type based on your purpose of travel.
  2. Check eligibility and gather required documents.
  3. Create an account on the IRCC portal and fill out the application form.
  4. Pay the visa fee online using a credit/debit card.
  5. Book a biometrics appointment (if required).
