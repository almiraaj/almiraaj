Types of Canada Visas

  • Visitor Visa: For tourism, family visits, or business trips (up to 6 months).
  • Study Permit: For international students enrolled in Canadian institutions.
  • Work Permit: For temporary employment opportunities.
  • Super Visa: For parents and grandparents of Canadian citizens or PRs (up to 5 years).
  • Permanent Residency: Express Entry, PNP, and Family Sponsorship programs.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Valid passport and travel documents
  • Proof of financial support
  • No criminal record
  • Medical exam (if required)
  • Intent to return to home country (for temporary visas)

Required Documents

  • Completed visa application form
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Bank statements or sponsor letter
  • Travel itinerary and accommodation proof
  • Letter of invitation (if applicable)

Visa Fees (CAD)

  • Visitor Visa: $100
  • Study Permit: $150
  • Work Permit: $155
  • Biometrics: $85 per person

*Fees are subject to change. Refer to the official site for updates.

Step-by-Step Visa Process

  1. Choose the appropriate visa type
  2. Gather all required documents
  3. Fill out the application form online via Canada.ca
  4. Pay the visa fee
  5. Book and attend biometrics appointment (if required)
  6. Wait for processing and receive your visa
