Marriage Certificate Attestation in Chennai

AL-MIRAAJ International offers fast, reliable, and government-approved marriage certificate attestation services in Chennai and across Tamil Nadu.

What is Marriage Certificate Attestation?

Marriage certificate attestation is a legal process that verifies the authenticity of a marriage certificate issued in India. This attestation is required when the document is to be used in a foreign country for purposes such as spouse visa applications, family sponsorship, immigration, or employment. Without proper attestation, your certificate may not be accepted by foreign authorities.

The process involves multiple levels of verification, including MEA Apostille, State Home Department, and the Embassy of the destination country.

Why is Attestation Required?

  • ✅ To apply for a spouse or dependent visa
  • ✅ For family reunification or residency abroad
  • ✅ To open joint bank accounts or apply for insurance overseas
  • ✅ To prove legal marital status in a foreign country

Step-by-Step Attestation Process

  1. Notary Attestation: Local verification by a notary public
  2. State Authentication: Tamil Nadu Home Department or SDM
  3. MEA Attestation: Ministry of External Affairs, India
  4. Embassy Attestation: Final verification by the destination country’s embassy
