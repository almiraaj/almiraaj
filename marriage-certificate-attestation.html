Marriage Certificate Attestation in Chennai

Government-approved marriage certificate attestation for UAE, Qatar, Oman, Kuwait, and beyond — trusted by families across Tamil Nadu.

What is Marriage Certificate Attestation?

It’s a mandatory legal procedure used to validate your Indian marriage certificate for use in foreign countries. This ensures that your certificate is accepted by embassies, consulates, and international organizations when applying for spouse visas, family sponsorships, joint banking, or immigration processes.

AL-MIRAAJ International helps individuals with every step, ensuring a smooth process with no document errors or embassy rejections.

Why is Attestation Required?

  • ✅ Spouse visa applications for GCC, EU, Canada, etc.
  • ✅ Family reunification and dependent sponsorships
  • ✅ Legal proof of marital status abroad
  • ✅ Opening joint accounts or insurance purposes overseas

Step-by-Step Attestation Procedure

  1. Notary Attestation – Local certification at the city level.
  2. State Authentication – Tamil Nadu Home Department or Sub-Divisional Magistrate.
  3. Ministry of External Affairs (MEA) – Central Government verification.
  4. Embassy Attestation – Final validation by the relevant foreign mission.
