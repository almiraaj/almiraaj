What is Marriage Certificate Attestation?

Marriage Certificate Attestation is the process of verifying the authenticity of a marriage certificate issued in India so it can be legally recognized abroad. This is essential for couples applying for spousal visas, residency, or accessing legal rights in foreign countries.

Why is Attestation Important?

Attestation ensures your marriage certificate is accepted by foreign governments, embassies, and legal institutions. It is crucial for immigration, joint property ownership, medical insurance, and spousal benefits.

Apostille vs Attestation

Apostille is used for countries under the Hague Convention and involves a single government stamp. Attestation is a multi-step process required for non-Hague countries involving state, MEA, and embassy verification.

  • Apostille : For Hague Convention countries (e.g., France, Germany)
  • Attestation : For non-Hague countries (e.g., UAE, Saudi Arabia)
