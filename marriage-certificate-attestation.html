"A marriage is not just a bond between two souls—it’s a legal promise that travels with you, wherever life takes you."

Marriage Certificate Attestation is essential for couples planning to move abroad, apply for spouse visas, or ensure their marriage is legally recognized internationally. Almiraaj International offers expert attestation services across Chennai, Trichy, Madurai, Coimbatore, and Tuticorin.

🌍 What Is Marriage Certificate Attestation?

It’s the process of verifying your marriage certificate through government and embassy channels to make it valid in foreign countries. Required for:

  • Spouse visa applications
  • Permanent residency
  • Family sponsorship
  • Legal claims or inheritance abroad

🛂 Why Is It Required?

"Love may be universal, but paperwork isn’t."

Every country has its own rules. Attestation ensures your certificate is genuine and accepted by foreign ministries and embassies.

🧭 Step-by-Step Attestation Process

  1. Notary Attestation – Local verification
  2. State Home Department / SDM – State-level authentication
  3. MEA Apostille / Attestation – Central government approval
  4. Embassy Legalization – Final stamp from the destination country’s embassy

📄 Documents Required

  • Original Marriage Certificate
  • Passport copies of both spouses
  • Authorization letter (if applicable)

🕒 Processing Time

Standard processing takes 7–15 working days. Express services available for urgent cases.
