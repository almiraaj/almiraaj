About Gamca Medical Online Appointment

A Gamca Medical Online Appointment refers to the process of scheduling a medical test conducted by the Gulf Cooperation Council Approved Medical Centers Association (GAMCA) for individuals planning to work or reside in Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) countries such as Saudi Arabia, the United Arab Emirates, Kuwait, Oman, Qatar, and Bahrain . To obtain a GAMCA medical appointment, individuals can book it online through various platforms that provide appointment scheduling services for GCC-approved medical tests . The appointment is typically allotted based on the availability of the medical center in the nearest city selected by the individual . During the GAMCA medical test, a comprehensive physical examination, blood tests, chest X-ray, and other relevant screenings are conducted to detect various medical conditions . The specific requirements and fees may vary depending on the country. For example, in Oman, the required documents include the GCC Medical Test Registration Slip, original passport, photocopy of passport, passport photos, and a COVID vaccination certificate
