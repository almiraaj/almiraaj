📜 What is Apostille for Albania?

Apostille is a legal certification under the Hague Convention of 1961 that authenticates Indian documents for use in Albania. It ensures that your documents are valid for legal, academic, and commercial purposes in Danish jurisdictions.

🛡️ Important Considerations

  • 📘 Certified translations into English or Danish may be required.
  • 🏫 Educational certificates might need institutional verification.
  • 📄 Commercial documents must be notarized and properly supported.
  • 👪 Personal documents should include originals and notarized copies.
