Saudi Visa Stamping Services

General Information

Planning to travel to Saudi Arabia? Begin with visa stamping at the Embassy in Delhi or Consulate in Mumbai. We assist with Work Visa, Family Visa, Residence Visa, and Business Visa stamping. Let us simplify the process for you with expert guidance and document support.

Our services include Saudi Embassy Attestation for educational, commercial, and personal documents—an essential step for legal recognition in Saudi Arabia. Learn more about our attestation process here.

For those embarking on the sacred journey of Islamic pilgrimage, we offer comprehensive Umrah Visa Services to ensure a smooth and spiritually fulfilling experience. Explore our Umrah packages and support here.

As part of the Saudi visa stamping process, a mandatory GCC-approved GAMCA medical examination is required. We help you schedule your medical appointment with authorized centers in Chennai,Trichy,Trivandrum,and Major available Cities . Book your slot easily here.

Our services are available across both almiraaj.com and almiraaj.in platforms—ensuring a seamless upgrade and unified experience for all our clients.

Resident / Dependant Visa

  • Original Passport + Old Passport (if available)
  • Visa Paper issued by Saudi Ministry
  • 4 Photos (4x6cm, white background)
  • GCC-approved Medical Report (appointment required)
  • Sponsor's Passport & Iqama Copy
  • Child: Polio Vaccine Certificate (Medical not required)
Marriage Certificate attestation is mandatory if spouse name is not on passport.

Family Visit Visa

  • Original Passport
  • Electronic Visa
  • Sponsor's Passport & Iqama Copy
  • 4 Photos (4x6cm, white background)
  • Baby’s Polio Certificate
Marriage Certificate attestation is mandatory if spouse name is not on passport.

Business / Commercial Visit Visa

  • Original Passport + Old Passport
  • 4 Photos (6x4cm, white background)
  • Last 3 Months Bank Statement (signed & sealed)
  • Invitation Letter via MOFA Portal
  • Attested Electronic Invitation Letter
  • Company Registration Certificate (Saudi & Indian)
  • Cover Letters from Applicant & Indian Company
  • Visa Recommendation Letter from Indian Chamber of Commerce
Additional documents may be requested by the Embassy.
