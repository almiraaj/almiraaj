Understanding QVP and SVP For Saudi Employment Visa Process:

"Saudi Arabia ensures that every professional and skilled worker entering the Kingdom is verified for competence and authenticity."

QVP ‚Äì Qualification Verification Program

Purpose: Verifies educational degrees and professional certifications.

Who needs it: Professionals in jobs that require formal qualifications (e.g., engineers, doctors, accountants, teachers).

Process:

  • Submit your degree certificates through the Saudi Ministry of Human Resources and Social Development‚Äôs portal.
  • Certificates are checked for authenticity and compatibility with the job role.
  • Once verified, you receive clearance for visa stamping.

Example: An engineer applying for a Saudi work visa must pass QVP to prove their degree is genuine.

Learn More about QVP ‚Äì Qualification Verification Program

SVP ‚Äì Skill Verification Program

Purpose: Tests practical skills for trade-based jobs.

Who needs it: Workers in hands-on professions (e.g., electricians, plumbers, drivers, mechanics).

Process:

  • Take a theory test and a practical test in your trade.
  • Conducted in approved centers in your home country.
  • Passing both tests confirms your eligibility for visa stamping.

Example: A plumber applying for a Saudi work visa must pass SVP to prove competence.
