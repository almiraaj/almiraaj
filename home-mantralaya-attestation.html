Introduction

About HOME Mantralaya Attestation

The administrative headquarters of the state government of Maharashtra is known as Mantralaya. It is a recognized and authorized department of the state government. Mantralaya Attestation is a state-level authentication process that validates the legitimacy of documents issued within Maharashtra. It is often a prerequisite for further attestation by the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA) or foreign embassies.

This attestation is applicable to a wide range of documents, including:

  • Educational certificates (degrees, diplomas, mark sheets)
  • Personal documents (birth, marriage, death certificates)
  • Legal affidavits and notarized papers
  • Employment and experience letters

Major purposes of Mantralaya Attestation include:

  • Visa applications for work, study, or residence abroad
  • Higher education admissions in foreign institutions
  • International employment verification
  • Immigration and migration documentation
  • Legal recognition of Indian documents overseas
The process typically begins with Notary Attestation, followed by verification at the Mantralaya, ensuring the document's authenticity before it proceeds to national or international authorities. Contact us to simplify your attestation journey with expert guidance and reliable service.
