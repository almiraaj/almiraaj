What is called HOME Mantralaya Attestation?

The administrative headquarters of the state government of Maharashtra is known as Mantralaya. It is a recognized and authorized department of the state government. Mantralaya Attestation is similar to Home Department Attestation and HRD Attestation.
Mantralaya attestation is an essential and mandatory procedure to ensure the authenticity and credibility of documents issued by the state of Maharashtra. This step is a prerequisite for document attestation by the MEA (Ministry of External Affairs) and various embassies. Certificate attestation in Mumbai cannot proceed without the involvement of the Mumbai Mantralaya.
