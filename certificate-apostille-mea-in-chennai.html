✅ Certificate Apostille Process Explained

Whether you're applying for a visa, studying abroad, or handling international business, apostille certification is essential. Here's how AL-MIRAAJ simplifies the process:

  1. Identify your document type: Educational, Personal, or Commercial Certificate.
  2. Complete initial verification (HRD, Home Department, or Chamber of Commerce) if required.
  3. Submit verified documents to the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA) for apostille stamping.
  4. Attach supporting documents such as a passport copy or valid ID proof.
  5. Processing time: Typically 3–5 working days for Chennai-based applicants.
