About Apostille

Apostille is the process of authenticating documents for international use under the Hague Convention of 1961. It certifies the genuineness of signatures, seals, and stamps on public documents, making them valid in all member countries.

Keywords: Apostille Services Chennai, MEA Apostille, Hague Convention Legalization

Why is Apostille Required?

Apostille ensures your certificates are recognized abroad for education, employment, migration, business, or legal claims. Without Apostille, documents may be rejected by foreign authorities, delaying your plans.
