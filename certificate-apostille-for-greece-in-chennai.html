🇬🇷 Why Greece Appeals to Indian Citizens

Greece is increasingly becoming a preferred destination for Indians—whether for academic pursuits, tourism, business expansion, or residency opportunities. Known for its rich heritage, stunning landscapes, and growing international education sector, Greece offers a balance of culture and opportunity.

Indians visit Greece for university admissions, international job opportunities, property investments, and tourism. With Schengen membership, Greece also provides gateway access to European travel and settlement plans.

Certificate Apostille is a mandatory requirement when submitting Indian documents for recognition in Greece. As part of the Hague Apostille Convention, Greece accepts documents only if they’re authenticated by India’s Ministry of External Affairs (MEA).

Documents such as birth and marriage certificates, educational degrees, affidavits, and legal declarations must carry an apostille stamp to be valid abroad.

Almiraaj International makes this process effortless—offering accurate, secure, and prompt apostille services for Greece through its offices in Chennai, Trichy, Madurai, and Tuticorin.
