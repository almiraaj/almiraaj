Singapore Visa Requirements & Fees

Indian applicants need a valid passport (6+ months), completed visa form, recent photos, covering letter, 6‑month bank statement, confirmed flight & hotel bookings, and invitation letter (if applicable).

Additional documents: Employed: Salary slips, IT returns, ID card. Self‑employed: Business license/MOA/partnership deed. Retired: Pension proof & bank statement. Student: School/college ID card. Visiting relatives: Invitation letter + inviter’s ID/address.

Key forms: Form 14A, Form V39A, AVA Authorisation Letter.

💰 Visa Fees: Embassy fee 30 SGD (~₹2100) + Service charges ₹1000. Including GST, total cost is approx. ₹3280. Travel itinerary, insurance, and accommodation support can be arranged additionally.
