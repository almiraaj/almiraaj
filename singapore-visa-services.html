A Singapore visa allows Indian nationals to travel for tourism, business, education, or family visits. Singapore Visa Requirements

To apply for a Singapore visa, Indian applicants typically need:

  • Valid Passport: Minimum 6 months validity
  • Visa Application Form: Duly filled and signed
  • Photographs: Passport-size, recent
  • Covering Letter: Purpose of visit and travel details
  • Bank Statement: Last 6 months
  • Hotel & Flight Bookings: Confirmed itinerary
  • Invitation Letter: If visiting family or attending business
