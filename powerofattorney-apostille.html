What is Power of Attorney Apostille?

A Power of Attorney (PoA) apostille is the official legalization of a document that authorizes someone to act on your behalf. Apostille is required when the document is to be used in a country that is part of the Hague Convention.

Whether you're an NRI managing property in India or a corporate client delegating authority abroad, apostilling your PoA ensures legal recognition overseas.
