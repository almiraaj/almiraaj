📜 About Apostille Certificate for Australia

Australia is a member of the Hague Convention of 1961, making the apostille process streamlined for Indian documents. Apostille is required for legal use of Indian certificates—such as birth, degree, and marriage documents—in Australia for education, employment, immigration, and business registration.

Once apostilled by India's Ministry of External Affairs, the documents are legally recognized in Australia without further embassy verification. It's fast, reliable, and internationally valid.

🛡️ Additional Requirements

  • 📘 Certified translation may be required.
  • 🏫 Educational certificates may need institutional verification.
  • 📄 Commercial documents should include notarization and supporting paperwork.
  • 👪 Personal documents must be submitted in original with verified copies.
