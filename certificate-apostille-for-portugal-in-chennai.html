📜 About Apostille Certificate for Portugal

To use Indian documents in Portugal, they must be apostilled. This process certifies the authenticity of the document’s signature and seal under the Hague Convention of 1961. Once apostilled, your documents are recognized by Portuguese authorities for legal, educational, and commercial purposes.

🛡️ Additional Requirements

  • 📘 Certified translation into English or Portuguese may be needed.
  • 🏫 Educational certificates may require institution verification.
  • 📄 Commercial documents should be notarized and properly supported.
  • 👪 Personal documents must include originals and notarized copies.
