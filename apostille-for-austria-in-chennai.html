Apostille Process for Austria

Apostille for Austria, also known as Germany legalization, is the process of verifying Indian documents so they are accepted in Austriay. This is essential for employment, education, business, or residency.

  • Choose document type — educational, personal, or commercial.
  • Get initial verification (Notary, Home Dept, SDM, COC, HRD, etc.).
  • Submit to MEA for apostille sticker/stamp.
  • Attach supporting documents (passport copy, ID).
  • Process completes in a few working days.
