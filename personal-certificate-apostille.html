✅ Personal Certificate Apostille in Chennai – Frequently Asked Questions

Personal Certificate Apostille is the process of authenticating individual documents such as birth, marriage, divorce, death, and medical certificates so they are legally valid in Hague Convention countries. It is required for students, families, professionals, and individuals migrating abroad.

Apostille ensures your personal certificates are accepted abroad without additional legalization. It simplifies processes like education admissions, marriage registration, migration, medical treatment, and family reunification in 100+ Hague Convention countries.

Apostille is valid in Hague Convention countries and requires only MEA authentication. Embassy Attestation is needed for non-Hague countries like UAE, Qatar, and Saudi Arabia, involving additional steps such as state verification and embassy approval.

Eligible documents include Birth Certificate, Marriage Certificate, Divorce Certificate, Death Certificate, and Medical Certificates. These are verified by the issuing authority or state home department before MEA apostille.

The timeline varies depending on document type and verification requirements. Generally, personal certificate apostille takes 2–5 working days if all documents are in order and submitted via authorized service providers.
