What is Personal Certificate Apostille?

Apostille is the authentication of personal documents for use in Hague Convention countries. Issued by the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA), India, it validates certificates like birth, marriage, divorce, and medical records internationally.

Keywords: Apostille for Personal Certificates, MEA Apostille India, Hague Convention Personal Documents

Why Apostille Matters for Individuals

Apostille ensures your personal documents are accepted abroad without further legalization. It simplifies processes like education, marriage, migration, medical treatment, and family matters across 100+ countries.

Keywords: Apostille for Migration, International Document Validation, MEA Apostille Chennai

Apostille vs Embassy Attestation

Apostille is valid in Hague Convention countries and requires only MEA authentication. Embassy attestation is required for non-Hague countries and involves additional steps like state verification and embassy approval.

  • Apostille : For Hague Convention countries (e.g., France, Germany, USA)
  • Attestation : For non-Hague countries (e.g., UAE, Qatar, Saudi Arabia)

MEA Apostille Process in India

The apostille process for personal certificates includes:

  1. Document Verification: By issuing authority or state home department
  2. Submission to MEA: Via authorized service provider
  3. Apostille Sticker: Issued by MEA with unique identification

To check whether your destination country accepts apostilled documents, refer to the Hague Convention Member Countries List.
