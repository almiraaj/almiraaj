About Divorce Certificate Apostille Attestation

A Divorce Decree / Certificate is the final ruling & judgment order from the court which states or provides a legal / official decision over termination of a marriage. It’s a legal agreement of duties & rights between two parties who are connected with divorce. Totally it’s a cancellation of a marriage.Divorce decree will be issued from court. It’s a court order. In some places it’s called family court. Divorce decree can be obtained in the same court where you have applied for divorce by paying the fees / charges applied by court. Once you receive the hard copies or true copies of divorce decree / certificate, you can follow the procedure below for Apostille / Attestation.There is often no disputing that divorce is an unpleasant word.However, there are sometimes exceptions. Sometimes, depending on the circumstances and circumstances of some people, divorce becomes essential for some people.At this time, if a divorced person wants to marry a foreigner, a divorce certificate is required, and the certificate is valid only if it has a government stamp, a stamp approved by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the embassy of the country.

Importance of Divorce Certificates Attestation.

Divorce certificate attestation is required in many cases. For example, if a divorced person wants to marry in another country, he needs a divorce certificate along with a certificate that he is currently living alone without being married to another person.Moreover those certificates will be accepted in other countries only if they have State Department,Ministry , Consular Attestation / Apostille. without attestation or apostille the certificate is not accepted in other countries
