Singapore Visa Services

Explore Singapore

  • Modern Cityscape: Discover iconic landmarks like Marina Bay Sands, Gardens by the Bay, and the Supertrees.
  • Cultural Diversity: Experience Singapore’s blend of Chinese, Malay, Indian, and Peranakan traditions through architecture, cuisine, and festivals.
  • Culinary Delights: Indulge in Singapore’s vibrant food scene, from hawker centers to Michelin-starred restaurants.
  • Shopping Paradise: Explore Orchard Road, Chinatown, and Little India for a unique shopping experience.
  • Sentosa Island: Enjoy attractions like Universal Studios Singapore, S.E.A. Aquarium, and pristine beaches.
  • Rich Heritage: Visit historic sites like Kampong Glam, Chinatown, and temples showcasing Singapore’s rich history.
  • Green Spaces: Relax at the Singapore Botanic Gardens or explore nature reserves like Bukit Timah.
  • Efficient Public Transport: Travel conveniently with Singapore’s well-connected MRT and bus network.
  • Clean & Safe: Singapore is known for its cleanliness and strict regulations ensuring a secure environment.
  • Festivals & Events: Celebrate lively traditions such as Chinese New Year, Thaipusam, and the Singapore Food Festival.

Singapore Visa Process - Required Documents

  • Original Passport valid for at least 6 months.
  • Completed Visa Application Form.
  • Two passport-size color photographs (35mm x 45mm).
  • Covering letter detailing travel and employment information.
  • Bank statement of the last six months.
  • Sponsor’s IC Copy & Passport Copy / Hotel Booking Receipt.
  • Proof of accommodation & round-trip ticket itinerary.
  • Employment & Education details, including leave letters.
  • Request Letter & V39A Form from Sponsor.

Note: Visa fees and regulations may change. The Embassy reserves the right to ask for additional documents or interviews. Submission does not guarantee visa approval.
