Process of Apostille for Germany

Apostille for Germany, also known as Germany legalization, is the process of verifying Indian documents so they are accepted in Germany. This is essential for employment, education, business, or residency.

  • Choose document type — educational, personal, or commercial.
  • Get initial verification according to the type of socuments(Notary,Home, SDM, COC,HRD, etc.).
  • Submit to MEA for apostille sticker/stamp.
  • Attach supporting documents (passport copy, ID).
  • Process completes in a few working days.
