Kuwait Work Visa Stamping in Coimbatore

Required Documents for Employment Visa Stamping:

  • Original Passport
  • Original Visa Paper issued by Kuwait Ministry
  • Original Police Clearance Certificate issued by Passport Office
  • Medical FIT Report from Kuwait Embassy approved clinic
  • Photograph
  • Covid Vaccine Certificate (Required for Delhi Submission)

Stamping Process Time:
Standard: 25 days
Express: 15 days
Superfast: 7 days

Kuwait Resident Visa Stamping in Coimbatore

Required Documents for Resident Visa Stamping:

  • Original Passport
  • Original Visa Paper issued by Kuwait Ministry
  • Original Police Clearance Certificate issued by Passport Office
  • Medical FIT Report from Kuwait Embassy approved clinic
  • Photograph
