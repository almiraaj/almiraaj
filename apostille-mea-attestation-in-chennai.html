Certificate Apostille Process – How It Works

The Apostille process is essential when you need your documents recognized internationally. Here's how it works in India, specifically for Chennai-based documentation:

  • Step 1 – Document Verification: Identify whether the document is personal (birth, marriage), educational (degree, transcript), or commercial (invoices, agreements). It must be original and unlaminated.
  • Step 2 – State-level Authentication: Documents are authenticated by relevant departments like HRD, Home Department, or Chamber of Commerce depending on the type.
  • Step 3 – MEA Apostille: Once verified, the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA) issues the Apostille sticker or stamp—valid across Hague Convention member countries.
  • Step 4 – Delivery: Documents are collected or delivered securely. AL-MIRAAJ ensures timely handover and tracking.
