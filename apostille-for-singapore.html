About Apostille

Apostille for Singapore legalizes Indian-origin documents under the Hague Convention. It is essential for employment, education, business, or immigration purposes. If you plan to travel, work, or study in Singapore, apostille ensures your documents are globally recognized.

✅
Why Apostille is Required

Singaporean authorities mandate apostilled documents to verify authenticity and compliance with international standards. It is required for visa processing, university admissions, employment contracts, and legal proceedings. Apostille ensures your paperwork is trusted and accepted without delays.

🔖
Apostille Process

The process includes: • Notary Attestation – local verification • State Authentication – SDM/State validation • MEA Apostille – final legalization by Ministry of External Affairs, India
