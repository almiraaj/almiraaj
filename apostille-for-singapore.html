❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is apostille mandatory for Singapore?
Yes, for any official document to be accepted in Singapore, apostille is required under the Hague Convention.

Can laminated documents be apostilled?
No. You must obtain a certified true copy from the issuing authority.

How long is an apostille valid?
Typically 6 months, but depends on the receiving authority.
