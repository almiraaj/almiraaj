Fast, secure, and MEA-approved document legalization for personal, educational, and commercial certificates.

What is SDM Attestation?

SDM stands for Sub-Divisional Magistrate, a legal department that operates independently from the main government. SDM Attestation is a type of document authentication process used during marksheet verification and certificate legalization in India.

This process is not direct‚Äîyou must follow a specific procedure to obtain verification from the SDM. It is commonly used for attesting personal documents such as birth certificates, marriage certificates, and educational documents like degree certificates and mark sheets.

SDM Attestation is typically performed in Delhi and serves as an alternative to State Home Department (SHD) or Human Resource Development (HRD) attestation. It is often required before proceeding to MEA (Ministry of External Affairs) and Embassy-level attestation.

Why Is SDM Attestation Required?

SDM Attestation is essential for legalizing documents before they are accepted by foreign governments and institutions. Whether you're applying for a residence visa, student visa, work visa, or employment abroad, your documents must be authenticated by the appropriate authorities.

While SDM Attestation is not the only route, it serves as a reliable alternative to State Home Department (SHD) and HRD Attestation. It is particularly useful when those departments are delayed or inaccessible. Once SDM attestation is complete, your documents can proceed to MEA (Ministry of External Affairs) and Embassy-level legalization.

How to Obtain SDM Attestation

SDM Attestation is issued only by the Sub-Divisional Magistrate in New Delhi. It cannot be obtained from other states. The process begins with Notary Attestation, followed by submission to the SDM office.
