Everything You Need to Know About SDM Attestation

Why Is SDM Attestation Required?

SDM Attestation is crucial for legalizing documents for foreign use—whether for a residence visa, student visa, or employment abroad. It’s a reliable alternative to SHD and HRD attestation, especially when those are delayed or inaccessible.

How to Obtain SDM Attestation

Issued only by the Sub-Divisional Magistrate in New Delhi, the process begins with Notary Attestation. AL-MIRAAJ simplifies it with pickup/drop, certificate verification, MEA coordination, and translation services.

SDM Attestation Procedure

Step 1: Notary Attestation
Step 2: Submission to SDM office
Step 3: Optional MEA/Embassy legalization
AL-MIRAAJ ensures smooth, compliant processing.

How Long Does It Take?

SDM Attestation typically takes 2 business days. For urgent needs, AL-MIRAAJ offers priority processing and fast-track delivery to meet tight deadlines.

Cost of SDM Attestation

Fees range from ₹1,500 to ₹5,000 depending on urgency, logistics, translation, and embassy coordination. Contact AL-MIRAAJ for a personalized quote.
