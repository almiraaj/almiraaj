Introduction

Taiwan, known for its blend of modern cities and scenic landscapes, has become an attractive destination for travelers from all around the world. The visa process for Taiwan is relatively straightforward and can be completed with minimal hassle. Here’s a brief overview of the different types of visas and the application process for Indian citizens wishing to visit Taiwan.

Visa Types and Purpose

  • Tourist Visa: Ideal for those planning to explore Taiwan's cultural heritage, stunning landscapes, and vibrant cities.
  • Business Visa: For individuals visiting Taiwan for business meetings, conferences, or other related activities.
  • Student Visa: Issued to students accepted by Taiwanese educational institutions.
  • Work Visa: For those who have secured employment in Taiwan.
  • Resident Visa: Necessary for those intending to stay in Taiwan for over six months.
  • Dependent Visa: For family members of individuals holding a work or resident visa.

Application Process

Step 1: Online Application

Complete the Taiwan visa application form online with accurate details including your name, passport information, travel itinerary, and more.

Step 2: Document Collection

Gather necessary documents such as a valid passport, recent photographs, flight details, proof of accommodation, and financial statements.

Step 3: Submission

Submit the completed form and required documents at a Taiwan diplomatic mission or through an authorized visa application agency.

Step 4: Processing and Approval

The visa application is processed within 3-7 days, though this may vary based on individual circumstances.

Step 5: Visa Issuance

Once approved, the visa is issued and can be downloaded and printed for your travels.

Key Points to Remember

  • Ensure all details on the application form match your prescribed documents.
  • Taiwan does not allow visa extensions or visa upgrades once you're inside the country on most eVisas.
  • Plan ahead as processing times might vary, and allow sufficient time for any additional documentation that might be requested.

Disclaimer: Visa approval is subject to the discretion of the Taiwan authorities and additional documentation may be required during the application process.

Taiwan Tourist Visa Requirements:

  • Visa application form (printed from online)
  • Both old and new passport in original
  • Passport valid for at least another six months from the intended date of entry into Taiwan
  • Two white background color passport size photos
  • Covering letter mentioning purpose of visit, designation of visitor, and the intended duration of stay
  • Tentative travel itinerary
  • Confirmed hotel booking in Taiwan
  • Personal bank statements for last six months original with bank seal signature
  • Confirmed round trip air tickets
  • Leave letter from the employer
  • Form 16 for 3 years or ITR for 3 years
  • Salary slip for the last 3 months

Taiwan Employment Visa

  1. Visa application form (printed from online)
  2. Both old and new passport in original
  3. Passport valid for at least another six months from the intended date of entry into Taiwan
  4. Two white background color passport size photos
  5. Covering letter mentioning purpose of visit, designation of visitor, and the intended duration of stay
  6. Duly signed contract, or proof of funding for research project (for post-doctoral position) or work permit issued by the Taiwanese authority in charge
  7. Offer letter/invitation letter from Taiwan (Such letters should be brought to the Taipei Economic and Cultural Center in Chennai as part of the application materials. Do not fax or send them to TECC in Chennai)
  8. Health certificate attested from the Ministry of External Affairs of India and authenticated by the TECC in Chennai - Optional
  9. Past six months bank statements
  10. Details of the contact person in Taiwan
  11. Confirmed air tickets
