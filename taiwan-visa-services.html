Taiwan Tourist Visa Requirements

To apply for a Taiwan Tourist Visa, Indian applicants typically need:

  • Online Visa Registration (printed & signed)
  • Original Passport (6+ months validity) + old passports
  • Last 3 months salary slips
  • Last 3 years IT returns
  • Company ID card copy
  • Employment letter with company seal
  • 2 passport-size photos (white background)
  • Covering letter with travel purpose
  • Tentative travel itinerary
  • Confirmed hotel booking
  • Bank letter addressed to TECC Chennai
  • Proof of residence (if applicable)
  • Confirmed round trip air tickets

Charges:

Description Normal (Approx. 12 Days) Urgent (Approx. 5-7 Days)
Single Entry Rs 9000/- Rs 11000/-

Note: Visa validity is at the discretion of the consulate.

Taiwan Business Visa Requirements

To apply for a Taiwan Business Visa, Indian applicants typically need:

  • Online Visa Registration (printed & signed)
  • Original Passport (6+ months validity) + old passports
  • 2 passport-size photos
  • Company ID card copy
  • Indian company cover letter
  • Personal covering letter
  • Invitation & Letter of Guarantee from Taiwan company
  • Company registration / IEC code
  • Company & personal IT returns (last 3 years)
  • Bank letters (personal & company) addressed to TECC Chennai
  • Contact person details in Taiwan
  • Proof of residence (if needed)
  • Round trip tickets & hotel booking
  • Schedule of stay

Charges:

Description Normal (Approx. 12 Days) Urgent (Approx. 5-7 Days)
Single Entry Rs 9000/- Rs 12000/-
Multiple Entry Rs 14000/- Rs 18000/-

Note: Visa validity is at the discretion of the consulate.
