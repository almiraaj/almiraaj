🔍 UAE Embassy Attestation – Process Explained

UAE Embassy Attestation is an official procedure to validate documents for use in the UAE. It involves verification by issuing authorities, government departments, and endorsement by the UAE Embassy or Consulate. This process is essential for employment, education, family visa, business setup, and residency purposes in the UAE.

📌 When Do You Need UAE Embassy Attestation?

  • Employment: Required for validating educational and work documents before UAE job applications.
  • Education: Academic certificates must be attested for university admissions in the UAE.
  • Family Visa: Birth and marriage certificates require attestation to obtain family visas.
  • Business Setup: Commercial documents must be legalized for company registration in the UAE.
  • Residency Permit: Personal documents must be attested for UAE residency applications.
  • Al Miraaj Expertise: With 25+ years of attestation experience and IATA accreditation, Al Miraaj offers trusted, compliant services.

📋 UAE Embassy Attestation Steps

  • ✅ Notary Attestation: Verifies signatures and identities for legal recognition in the UAE.
  • 🏛 HRD Attestation: Confirms educational document authenticity for study or work.
  • 📑 SDM Attestation: Alternative to State Home Department for personal document verification.
  • 🖋 MEA Attestation: Ministry-level validation of documents for UAE-bound migration.
  • 👪 Home Mantralaya Attestation: Verifies birth, marriage, and family records at state level.
  • 📦 COC Attestation: Chamber of Commerce confirms commercial documentation for trade and business setup.
