Chinese Visa Process - Explained

Explore China with the Right Visa for Your Journey

  • Tourist Visa (L Visa): Ideal for tourism and sightseeing in China. Valid for 30–60 days per entry, may include single, double, or multiple entries. Requires return flight and hotel booking proof.
  • Business Visa (M Visa): Issued for trade and commercial visits. Requires an invitation letter from a Chinese partner. Validity may range from 30 days to 1 year.
  • Student Visa (X Visa): For students joining Chinese institutions. X1 for over 180 days, X2 for short-term study. Requires visa application form (JW201/202) and admission letter.
  • Work Visa (Z Visa): Granted to those with job offers in China. Requires employment license or permit. Convert to residence permit within 30 days of arrival.
  • Family Reunion Visa (Q Visa): For families of Chinese citizens or permanent residents. Q1 for long stay, Q2 for short visits. Invitation letter and proof of relationship required.
