Understanding Kuwait Visa Stamping & Embassy Attestation

Kuwait visa stamping is the process of affixing a visa to a traveler's passport in India—typically for employment or family reunion—after receiving visa approval from a sponsor in Kuwait.

Key steps include obtaining a Police Clearance Certificate (PCC), undergoing a GAMCA-approved medical examination, and submitting the application to the Kuwait Embassy in India, which stamps the passport to authorize entry.

Required documents include the passport, original visa, photographs, and for children, a polio vaccination certificate.

Kuwait Embassy attestation is a legalization process where a Kuwait embassy stamp verifies the authenticity of your documents for use in Kuwait, typically for employment, study, or residency.

💼 Kuwait Employment Visa Stamping

For professionals traveling to Kuwait for work, employment visa stamping is mandatory. We assist with documentation, medical coordination, and embassy submission.

📋 Required Documents:
  • Original Passport
  • Visa Copy issued by Kuwait employer
  • GAMCA Medical Report
  • Police Clearance Certificate (PCC)
  • Passport-size Photos
  • Vaccination Certificate
🔄 Process Overview:
  1. Collect all required documents
  2. Schedule GAMCA medical test
  3. Submit PCC and medical report
  4. Submit documents to Kuwait Embassy
  5. Receive stamped visa

🏠 Kuwait Resident Visa Stamping

For family joining or dependent visas, resident visa stamping ensures legal entry into Kuwait. We guide you through every step of the process.

📋 Required Documents:
  • Original Passport
  • Resident Visa Copy
  • Medical Certificate (Polio vaccine for children)
  • PCC (Not required for children)
  • Photographs
  • Vaccination Certificate
🔄 Process Overview:
  1. Obtain visa from Kuwait sponsor
  2. Complete medical and PCC formalities
  3. Prepare all documents
  4. Submit to embassy for stamping
  5. Collect stamped passport
