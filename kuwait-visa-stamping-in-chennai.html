About Kuwait Resident Visa Stamping

Kuwait visa stamping refers to the process of obtaining an official visa stamp in a passport to enter Kuwait. It is a necessary step for individuals who want to visit or work in Kuwait. The visa stamping process typically involves submitting the required documents to the appropriate authorities, such as the Kuwaiti Embassy or Consulate, and paying the necessary fees. The specific documents required may vary depending on the type of visa being applied for, but typically include a valid passport, passport-sized photographs, and other supporting documents such as a medical certificate or police clearance certificate. The visa stamp is affixed to the passport, indicating that the individual has been granted permission to enter Kuwait for a specified period of time and for a specific purpose.

Step by Step Process For Kuwait Visa Stamping - Resident | Family Joining
  • Process 1: A valid entry visa issued by the Kuwait Ministry must be obtained from Kuwait.
  • Process 2: PCC - Police Clearance Certificate (Not required for children).
  • Process 3: Medical appointment (Not required for children).
  • Process 4: Medical FIT (For child visa, Polio vaccine is required).
  • Process 5: Gather all the required documents for Kuwait visa stamping: passport, photos, visa, PCC, medical, vaccine, etc.
  • Process 6: After your Kuwait visa is stamped, check it and be ready to depart by the deadline with all required documents.

However, an approximate processing time of 15 to 25 days is taken to process the Kuwait visa stamping procedure, subject to the embassy/consulate restrictions/terms and conditions. The duration may vary depending on the number of documents and other circumstances.
