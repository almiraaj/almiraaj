Some Information About philippines. Tourism:

  • Stunning Beaches: From the famous White Beach in Boracay to the serene shores of Palawan, the Philippines boasts some of the best beaches in the world..
  • Vibrant Festivals: Experience the colorful Sinulog Festival in Cebu or the lively Ati-Atihan Festival in Kalibo.
  • Rich Cultural Heritage: Explore historical sites like Intramuros in Manila and the Banaue Rice Terraces, which showcase the country's deep-rooted history and traditions.
  • Diverse Marine Life: Dive into the Tubbataha Reefs Natural Park or swim with whale sharks in Donsol.
  • Delicious Cuisine: Enjoy local delicacies like adobo, sinigang, and lechon, along with a variety of tropical fruits.
  • Warm Hospitality: Filipinos are known for their friendliness and hospitality, making visitors feel welcome and at home.
  • Adventure Activities: From trekking Mount Pinatubo to exploring the underground river in Puerto Princesa, there's no shortage of adventure.
  • Affordable Travel: Compared to other destinations, the Philippines offers a more budget-friendly travel experience.
  • Beautiful Islands: With over 7,000 islands, each with its own unique charm, there's always something new to discover.
  • Dynamic Cities: Visit the bustling metropolis of Manila or the modern and vibrant city of Cebu for a mix of urban excitement and cultural experiences.

Philippines Visa Process Required Documents

  • Original passport with at least 6 months validity, 3 blank pages + any old passports.
  • Visa Application form.
  • 2 recent colour photographs.
  • Original covering letter mentioning Applicant Name, Passport No, Travel dates, details of persons travelling together, Stay details in Philippines, Employment details Mobile number and email ID of the applicant,The letter must be filled and signed with a black pen.
  • Bank statement for the last six months, signed by a bank official
  • Supporting Financial Documents such as Fixed Deposits, Property Investments, Other Investments etc.
  • You must have proof of the accommodation & Ticket Itinerary Round Trip Must.
  • Working Details Leave Letter,Education Details Certificates/Leave Letter
  • Applicants contact no and email address.
