About Apostille Certificate For France

What is an apostille certificate for France in India?An Apostille is basically a certification of document issued by a legal or registered authority in a country. If a document has been Apostilled it means that the document is verified and it is legal to use in the country to which it has been Apostilled.In accordance with the Convention, in order for Indian documents to be valid in France, they must bear an apostille certificate confirming the capacity of the government official signing the document, authenticity of the signature and, where appropriate, of the seal or stamp on the document.

Why Apostille for France ?

In international law, Apostille/Attestation is the process of certifying a document so a foreign country’s legal system will recognize it. The process is used routinely in international commerce. We can assist you getting apostille/attestation for France almost all type of certificates / documents issued by India.


  • If you have a job in France and you are applying for a Work Visa it is mandatory to submit your PCC (Police clearance Certificate), Degree certificate and Experience Letter with Apostille.
    • If You are Trying to register your marriage in France or applying for a Francen dependent visa for your wife and kids you need to apostille Marriage certificate and birth certificate of all the candidates.
      • As an Indian citizen if you are Marrying in France then you need to get an Apostille for France on your single status certificate.
        • To open a branch or conduct any business in France you need to get an apostille for France on documents such as GST Certificate, Registration Certificate, AOA(Article of Association), and MOU(Memorandum of Understanding).
          • To enroll in a France university for higher education it is mandatory for you to get an apostille on the documents such as Degree Certificate and Transcript.
