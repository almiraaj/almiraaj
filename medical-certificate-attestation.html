What is Medical Certificate ?

A written declaration from a certified doctor confirming the medical test of a patient is called a medical certificate and can be used as a ‘sick report’. Medical Certificate needs to be authenticated and verified by a legal authority to prove its gentility.A medical certificate, also known as a doctor's certificate, is a written statement from a physician or another medically qualified healthcare provider that confirms the result of a medical examination of a patient . It serves various purposes, such as providing a sick note (also known as a fit note ) to document an employee's unfitness for work or serving as evidence of a health condition Medical certificates can also be used in other contexts, such as excusing a student's absence from school due to illness .
