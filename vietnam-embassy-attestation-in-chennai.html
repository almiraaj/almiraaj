🔍 Vietnam Embassy Attestation – Process Explained

Vietnam Embassy Attestation is an official procedure to validate documents for use in the Vietnam. It involves verification by issuing authorities, government departments, and endorsement by the Vietnam Embassy or Consulate. This process is essential for employment, education, family visa, business setup, and residency purposes in the Vietnam.

📌 When Do You Need Vietnam Embassy Attestation ?

  • Employment: Required for validating educational and work documents before Vietnam job applications.
  • Education: Academic certificates must be attested for university admissions in the Vietnam.
  • Family Visa: Birth and marriage certificates require attestation to obtain family visas.
  • Business Setup: Commercial documents must be legalized for company registration in the Vietnam.
  • Residency Permit: Personal documents must be attested before Vietnam residency applications.

📋 Vietnam Embassy Attestation Steps

  • ✅ Notary Attestation: Verifies signatures and identities for legal recognition in the Vietnam.
  • 🏛 HRD Attestation: Confirms educational document authenticity for study or work.
  • 📑 SDM Attestation: Alternative to State Home Department for personal document verification.
  • 🖋 MEA Attestation: Ministry-level validation of documents before Vietnam-bound migration.
  • 👪 Home Mantralaya Attestation: Verifies birth, marriage, and family records at state level.
  • 📦 COC Attestation: Chamber of Commerce confirms commercial documentation for trade and business setup.
