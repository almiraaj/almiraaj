Process of Vietnam Embassy Attestation

  • Vietnam embassy attestation , also known as Vietnam embassy legalization, is a process that involves verifying the authenticity of documents issued in India so that they are accepted as valid in Vietnam. This attestation is often required for various purposes, such as employment, education, business, or residency in Vietnam. The process typically involves the following steps

    • Notarization: to get Vietnam Embassy Attestation ,The document must first be notarized by a notary public or an authorized government official in the country where the document was issued. This step ensures the authenticity of the document.
      • Authentication: After notarization, the document needs to be authenticated by the relevant government department either Home Department State Govt authority or Home-Mantralaya , or MEA - Ministry of External Affairs in India. So The authentication confirms the authority of the notary public or official who notarized the document.
        • Vietnam Embassy Attestation: Once the document has been attested by the issuing country's authorities, it needs to be attested by the Vietnami embassy or consulate in that country. This step is crucial to establish the document's validity for use in Vietnam.
          • Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MOFA) Attestation in Vietnam: In some cases, depending on the type of document and the purpose, the attested document might also need to be further attested by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs in Vietnam.
