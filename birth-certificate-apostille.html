What is a Birth Certificate?

An official document that records your birth details — essential for identity, legal, and international purposes.

Benefits of Having One

  • Proof of identity and age
  • Required for passport, visa, education
  • Legal claims and inheritance

What is an Apostille?

An international certification under the Hague Convention that authenticates documents for use abroad.

Why Birth Certificates Need Apostille or Attestation

  • Immigration and citizenship processes
  • Employment verification overseas

Step-by-Step Process

Birth Certificate Apostille

  1. Notary authentication
  2. State Home Department verification
  3. MEA Apostille stamp

Embassy Attestation

  1. Notary authentication
  2. State Home Department verification
  3. MEA attestation
  4. Embassy attestation of the destination country
