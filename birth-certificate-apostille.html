What is a Birth Certificate?

A birth certificate is an official document issued by the government that records the birth details of an individual, including name, date of birth, place of birth, and parentage. It is the foundational identity document for every citizen.

Benefits of Having a Birth Certificate

  • Proof of identity and age
  • Required for school admissions and government services
  • Essential for passport and visa applications
  • Legal evidence for inheritance and property claims
  • Mandatory for marriage registration and voter ID

What is an Apostille?

An apostille is a certificate issued under the Hague Convention that authenticates the origin of a public document. It simplifies international document legalization for member countries.
