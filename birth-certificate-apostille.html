🌍 What is Birth Certificate Apostille?

A birth certificate apostille is an official authentication issued by the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA) in India. It validates your birth certificate for use in Hague Convention countries—essential for NRIs, students, and professionals seeking legal recognition abroad.
