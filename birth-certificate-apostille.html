What is Birth Certificate Apostille?

An apostille is a type of international authentication issued for documents that are to be used in countries that are members of the Hague Convention of 1961. A birth certificate apostille confirms that your document is genuine, valid, and accepted overseas.

Apostille Process for Birth Certificate

  1. Collection of original birth certificate and passport copy.
  2. State Home Department or SDM verification (if required).
  3. Submission to the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA), India.
  4. Sticker Apostille is applied and sealed by MEA.
  5. Delivery of apostilled document via courier or in-person.

Types of Attestation for Birth Certificates

  • Apostille (Hague Convention): For use in Hague member countries.
  • Embassy Attestation: For non-Hague countries like UAE, Qatar, or Kuwait.
  • HRD/Home Dept Attestation: Pre-requisite in some Indian states.

Required Documents

  • Original Birth Certificate (in English or translated).
  • Clear photocopy of passport (first & last page).
  • ID proof (if required).
  • Authorization letter (if submitted via agent).

Why Do You Need Birth Certificate Apostille?

  • Child admission or schooling abroad.
  • Visa applications (student, dependent, family visa).
  • Citizenship or immigration processing.
  • Marriage registration in a foreign country.
  • Proof of family relationship overseas.
