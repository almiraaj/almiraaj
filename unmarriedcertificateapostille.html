Understanding Unmarried Certificate Apostille:

A Single Status Certificate is a legal document confirming that someone is not currently married. It's often required for:

  • Marriage Abroad: Verifies your single status for international marriage procedures.
  • Marriage License Applications: Supports eligibility for marriage in foreign jurisdictions.
  • Immigration Documentation: Proves marital status when applying for family-related immigration.

Certificate for Indian to get married with a foreigner | Updated:

If you're an Indian citizen planning to marry a foreign national, an Unmarried Certificate is a crucial document that confirms your single status. It is often required by foreign authorities to ensure legal eligibility for marriage abroad. To make this certificate legally valid outside India, it must be authenticated through an apostille under the Hague Apostille Convention.
