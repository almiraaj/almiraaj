What Is Power of Attorney Apostille & Why It Matters

Legal Representation Abroad

A Power of Attorney allows someone to act on your behalf for property, finance, or legal matters internationally.

Apostille vs. Embassy Attestation

Hague Convention countries accept apostille; others like UAE require embassy attestation.

POA Attestation Process in India

✅ Notary Attestation

Initial verification of signature and identity.

✅ Home Department

Required for personal POAs before MEA processing.

✅ SDM Attestation

Alternative to Home Department for faster embassy approval.

✅ MEA Apostille

Final legalization for Hague Convention countries.
