What is an apostille certificate for Russia in India?An Apostille is basically a certification of document issued by a legal or registered authority in a country. If a document has been Apostilled it means that the document is verified and it is legal to use in the country to which it has been Apostilled.In accordance with the Convention, in order for Indian documents to be valid in Russia, they must bear an apostille certificate confirming the capacity of the government official signing the document, authenticity of the signature and, where appropriate, of the seal or stamp on the document.
