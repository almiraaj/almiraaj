Frequently Asked Questions

What is certificate attestation?

It’s the process of verifying a document’s authenticity by authorized government departments to make it legally valid abroad.

Why is attestation required?

It’s mandatory for using Indian documents in foreign countries—for education, employment, migration, or business purposes.

How long does the attestation process take?

It depends on the document type and destination country, but we aim to complete it within 7–10 working days.

What documents are needed?

Original certificate, passport copy, visa (if applicable), and any embassy-specific forms or ID proofs.

Is doorstep delivery available?

Yes! Once attestation is complete, we offer secure delivery to your address or you can collect it from our office.
