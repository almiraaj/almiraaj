Australia Visa Services – Tourist & Business

Planning a trip to Australia? Whether you're visiting for leisure or business, understanding the visa process is key. Australia offers multiple visa subclasses tailored to different travel needs. Our team at AL-MIRAAJ International ensures smooth documentation and expert guidance for your application.

If you're submitting Indian documents for use in Australia—whether for education, employment, immigration, or business—you'll need them apostilled. As a member of the Hague Convention, Australia accepts apostilled documents without further embassy legalization. Click here to learn more about Apostille for Australia and ensure your certificates are legally recognized abroad.

✨ About the Australia Tourist Visa

The Australia Tourist Visa (Subclass 600) allows short-term visits for tourism or business visitor activities. Applicants must not engage in paid work. Visa durations range from 3 months to 1 year and may be extended in rare cases.

Australia’s stunning coastlines, vibrant cities, and natural wonders make it a top destination. Other visa options include Subclass 601 (Electronic Travel Authority), 651 (eVisitor), and working holiday visas (417/462).

📋 Tourist Visa Requirements
  • ✅ Original Passport
  • ✅ 4 Photos (white background)
  • ✅ Filled 1419 Visa Form
  • ✅ Covering Letter
  • ✅ Employment/Business Proof
  • ✅ Hotel Confirmation + Itinerary
  • ✅ Bank Statement (6 months)
  • ✅ Income Tax Papers (3 years)
  • ✅ Pay Slips (3 months)
  • ✅ Employer Leave Certificate
  • ✅ School Leave Certificate (for children)
  • ✅ Sponsorship Documents (if visiting relatives)
💼 About the Business Visa

The Australia Business Visa (also under Subclass 600) is designed for short-term business activities such as attending meetings, conferences, or negotiations. It does not permit employment or long-term business operations.

Applicants must demonstrate genuine business intent and provide supporting documents from both Indian and Australian entities.

📑 Business Visa Requirements
  • ✅ 2 Colored Photos (white background)
  • ✅ Covering Letter on Company Letterhead
  • ✅ Invitation Letter from Australia
  • ✅ Business Proof & Registration
  • ✅ Bank Statements – Personal & Company (6 months)
  • ✅ Income Tax Returns – Personal & Company (3 years)
  • ✅ Pay Slips (3 months)
  • ✅ Health Insurance (mandatory for applicants above 70)
Note: Visa fees and guidelines may change without prior notice. The Embassy/Consulate may request additional documents or an interview. Submission of documents does not guarantee visa approval.
