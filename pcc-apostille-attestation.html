What is PCC Apostille?

A Police Clearance Certificate (PCC) apostille is an official authentication issued by the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA) in India. It validates your PCC for use in countries that are part of the Hague Convention.

Accepted Countries

  • Germany, France, Italy, Netherlands
  • Japan, South Korea, New Zealand
  • Spain, Switzerland, Belgium, and more

Why Apostille is Needed

  • Employment abroad
  • Immigration and residency applications
  • Visa processing
  • Legal background verification

Requirements

  • Passport copy
  • Government-issued ID proof
