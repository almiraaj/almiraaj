Visitor & Tourism (B1/B2)

Short‑term travel for tourism, family visits, conferences, or business meetings. Not permitted to work.

  • Stay: Typically up to 6 months
  • Form: DS‑160 + interview
  • Docs: Passport, photo, itinerary, finances

Student (F‑1/M‑1)

Academic (F‑1) or vocational (M‑1) study at SEVP‑approved schools. Optional Practical Training may apply.

  • I‑20: Issued by school
  • SEVIS fee: Pay before interview
  • Proof: Funds, academics, intent to return

Work & Business (H‑1B/L‑1)

Specialty occupation (H‑1B) or intra‑company transfer (L‑1). Employer petition required before stamping.

  • Petition: USCIS approval (I‑797)
  • Docs: Offer letter, qualifications
  • Dependents: H‑4/L‑2 options

Exchange & Other (J, O, E, etc.)

Programs for research, training, extraordinary ability, investors, and more—each with specific criteria.

  • J‑1: DS‑2019 sponsor form
  • O‑1: Evidence of achievements
  • E‑2: Substantial investment
