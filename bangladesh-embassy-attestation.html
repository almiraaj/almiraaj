What is Bangladesh Embassy Attestation?

Bangladesh Embassy Attestation is the official process of verifying and legalizing documents for use in Bangladesh. This includes authentication by the issuing authority, state departments, and final endorsement by the Bangladesh Embassy or Consulate. It is essential for employment, education, family visas, business setup, and residency in Bangladesh.

Top 5 Reasons to Get Bangladesh Embassy Attestation

  • Employment in Bangladesh: Required for validating educational and professional documents.
  • Higher Education: Academic certificates must be attested for university admissions.
  • Family Visa: Marriage and birth certificates need attestation for family relocation.
  • Business Setup: Commercial documents must be authenticated for legal operations.
  • Residency Permit: Personal documents must be attested to obtain residency status.

🔏 Notary Attestation

Verifies the authenticity of documents, signatures, and identities with a notary seal. Prevents fraud and ensures legal recognition.

🏛️ HRD Attestation

State HRD verifies educational documents issued by recognized institutions. Required for studying or working in Bangladesh.

📜 SDM Attestation

Sub-Divisional Magistrate attestation is an alternative to State Home Department verification. Adds credibility for international use.

🌐 MEA Attestation

The Ministry of External Affairs validates documents for global use, including employment, education, and migration to Bangladesh.

🏠 Home Mantralaya Attestation

State Home Department verifies personal documents like birth and marriage certificates. Required for family visas and legal recognition.

📦 COC Attestation

The Chamber of Commerce attests commercial documents like invoices and certificates of origin. Crucial for international trade and business.
