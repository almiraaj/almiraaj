About Japan Tourist Visa

Introduction

If you intend to visit Japan for tourism purposes, you will likely need a Japan Tourist Visa. Japan is one of the most-visited countries in the world, receiving millions of tourists every year. When you submit a Japanese Tourist Visa application, you need to have several supporting documents. Japan is well known for its beauty, gardens, sanctuaries, sustenance, festivals, historic locations, amusement parks, and more. Thousands of tourists visit Japan every year. For Indians to visit Japan, they need a Japanese visa, a short-term visa, or a temporary guest visa.

Japan Tourist Visa Requirements
  • Original passport valid for at least six months with at least one empty page for the visa.
  • One online application form with all columns duly filled by the applicant with a signature.
  • 3 recent photographs (4.5 cm x 4.5 cm) with white background.
  • Original covering letter addressed to "The Visa Officer, Consulate General of Japan, Chennai".
  • Hotel confirmation as per travel dates for all family members.
  • Day-to-day itinerary as per hotel confirmation.
  • 3 years IT returns and 6 months bank statement.
  • Bonafide certificate from school or college with ID card copy (for students).
  • Appointment order copy, salary slips for last 3 months, and leave sanction letter from employer (if employed).
  • 3 years IT returns and 6 months bank statements of the company (if self-employed).
  • Proof of ownership of business (e.g., memorandum articles of association or partnership).
  • Registration certificate of the company or TNGST/CST.
  • Copy of return air ticket.
If Friend or Relatives in Japan
  • Letter of invitation from Japan in original.
  • List of applicants visiting Japan.
  • Guarantee letter from the inviter.
  • Passport copy along with valid resident permit visa copy.
Important Note
Only passports issued from Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Kerala, and Puducherry can be applied in CHENNAI. Minimum 5 working days from the date of submission to the consulate. The Consulate General reserves the right to ask for additional documents, may require additional days to process the visa, and issuance/rejection of the visa is at consulate discretion.
Japan Business Visa Requirements
  • Original passport valid for at least six months with at least one empty page for the visa.
  • One Visa Application Form with all columns filled and signed by the applicant.
  • 3 recent photographs (4.5 cm x 4.5 cm) with white background.
  • Covering letter from business letterhead in detail with hotel name and contact number, addressing to "The Visa Officer, Consulate General of Japan, Chennai".
  • Invitation letter in original from Japan with contact number of the person inviting.
  • Guarantee letter in original from Japan.
  • Schedule of Stay from Japan.
  • Overview of the Japanese company.
  • List of applicants (if more than one) from the Japanese company.
  • Hotel confirmation from Japan.
  • Appointment or re-designation letter.
  • Salary slips for the last 3 months.
  • Company’s registration copy.
  • Bank statements for the last 3 months (personal & company).
  • Income tax for the last 3 years (personal & company).
  • Applicant PAN card copy.
  • Confirmed air ticket/itinerary as per schedule of stay.
  • Proof of residence for other region passport holders.
Important Note
Only passports issued from Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Kerala, and Puducherry can be applied in CHENNAI. The Consulate General reserves the right to ask for additional documents, may require additional days to process the visa, and issuance/rejection of the visa is at consulate discretion. Minimum 5 working days from the date of submission to the consulate.
