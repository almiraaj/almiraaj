Major Types of Japan Visa Services

Almiraaj International assists with multiple categories of Japan visas including tourist, business, and short-term visit visas. Each type requires specific documentation such as passport validity, application forms, photographs, and financial proof to ensure smooth processing at the Consulate General of Japan in Chennai.

Tourist & Short-Term Visit Visas

For leisure travel, visiting relatives, or meeting friends, Japan tourist visas require documents such as hotel confirmations, day-to-day itineraries, proof of funds, and invitation letters (if applicable). Almiraaj ensures your application is complete and compliant with embassy requirements.

Keywords: Japan Tourist Visa India, Visiting Relatives Japan, Short-Term Stay Visa

Business Visa Services

Japan business visas are essential for corporate meetings, trade visits, and professional collaborations. Requirements include invitation letters from Japanese companies, guarantee letters, company registration documents, and financial statements. Almiraaj guides applicants through every step for a hassle-free process.

Keywords: Japan Business Visa Chennai, Corporate Visa Japan, Embassy Visa Documentation

Application & Processing Timeline

Japan visa applications generally take a minimum of 5 working days for processing at the Consulate General of Japan, Chennai. Almiraaj ensures timely submission, acknowledgement, and delivery of documents, with options for doorstep delivery once the visa is issued.

Keywords: Japan Visa Processing Time, Consulate General of Japan Chennai, Visa Application Support

Required Documentation

Japan visa applications require a valid passport, completed visa application form, recent photograph, itinerary, proof of financial stability, and supporting documents such as invitation letters or proof of relationship. Almiraaj guides applicants to prepare and organize all necessary paperwork to avoid delays or rejections.

Keywords: Japan Visa Documents, Japan Visa Requirements Chennai, Visa Application Checklist

Family & Dependent Visa Support

For applicants visiting spouses, children, or relatives in Japan, Almiraaj provides specialized assistance with dependent and family visit visas. This includes preparing kinship proof, invitation letters, and guarantee documents to ensure smooth approval by the Consulate General of Japan.
