What is Thailand Embassy Attestation?

Thailand Embassy Attestation is the official process of validating Indian documents so they are legally accepted by Thai authorities. This attestation is mandatory for employment visas, higher education, business incorporation, medical treatment, family visas, and long-term residency permits in Thailand.

Why Do You Need Attestation for Thailand?

Attestation ensures your documents are recognized by the Government of Thailand, Thai employers, universities, and legal authorities. Without proper attestation, your applications for work, study, or residency may face delays or rejection.

Apostille vs Thailand Embassy Attestation

Apostille is valid for Hague Convention countries. Thailand is a Hague Convention member, so Apostille is accepted for most documents such as educational, personal, and PCC.
