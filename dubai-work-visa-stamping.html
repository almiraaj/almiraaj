🛂 UAE Employment Visa Stamping Procedures

Effective from 14 Dec 2020, UAE visa processing has commenced at the UAE Consulate in Trivandrum, Kerala. This applies to all Emirates including Abu Dhabi, Ajman, Sharjah, Dubai, Fujairah, Ras Al Khaimah, and Umm Al Quwain.

Once your UAE employer confirms that your work visa has been applied, you can schedule your GAMCA Medical at an approved clinic. Original visa documents are not required for medical or stamping procedures.

Almiraaj offers complete assistance for UAE Employment Visa Stamping through the Trivandrum Consulate. Our team ensures smooth coordination, document preparation, and timely updates throughout the process.

📄 UAE Embassy Attestation Services

UAE Embassy Attestation is a mandatory procedure for validating Indian documents for use in the UAE. It involves multiple steps including Notary, HRD/Home Department, MEA, and final endorsement by the UAE Embassy. This process is essential for:

  • ✅ Employment visa processing
  • ✅ Higher education admissions
  • ✅ Family visa applications
  • ✅ Business setup and trade
  • ✅ Residency permits

Almiraaj International provides end-to-end support for UAE Embassy Attestation, ensuring compliance and timely delivery. Learn more about the process and document categories by visiting UAE Embassy Attestation in Chennai.

Step-by-Step Process for Dubai Visa Stamping 🇦🇪

Step 1: Receiving Your Entry Permit/e-Visa

Obtain a valid UAE entry permit or e-visa issued by UAE immigration. Usually arranged by your employer or sponsor. Ensure all details match your passport.

Step 2: Undergoing the Medical Fitness Test (GAMCA)

Book your GAMCA medical test online at an approved center. We assist with booking—just send your passport & Aadhaar (Service fee: ₹1200). Medical fees: ₹7500 approx.

book your GAMCA appointment .

Step 3: Medical Certificate Attestation

Your medical certificate will be attested by the UAE Consulate and UAE MOFA. This is a mandatory step before visa stamping begins.

Step 4: Visa Stamping Application & Biometrics

Submit your visa stamping application along with attested medical certificate. Biometrics and stamping are done at the UAE Consulate in Trivandrum.

Step 5: Passport Collection

Processing time: 3–5 working days. Once stamping is complete, we notify you and courier your passport—no need to stay in Trivandrum.
