South Korea Visa Services

Introduction:

South Korea, officially known as the Republic of Korea, is a vibrant and dynamic country located in East Asia. With a rich cultural heritage and a thriving economy, South Korea has made significant advancements in various fields. One of the notable features of South Korea is its technological prowess.

Additionally, South Korea has preserved its traditional culture and historical landmarks such as the Changdeokgung Palace and the Jongmyo Shrine, which reflect its rich history and architectural brilliance. South Korea's cuisine, including dishes like kimchi, bibimbap, and bulgogi, is globally acclaimed for its flavors and health benefits.

About the Visa Process:

The visa process for Indian nationals traveling to South Korea involves several steps. First, applicants complete the visa application form and submit it along with supporting documents such as a valid passport, recent photographs, flight itinerary, hotel booking, and proof of financial stability. Once reviewed by the South Korean embassy or consulate, a visa is issued if approved. The process typically takes 10-12 working days.

South Korea Tourist Visa Requirements:

  • Complete the online application form.
  • Provide the original passport and any previous passports.
  • Submit two recent photographs (35x45 mm, white background, glossy finish, 80% face image).
  • Present a covering letter addressed to the Visa Officer at the Embassy of South Korea in Chennai.
  • Present an employment certificate or a student bonafide certificate.
  • Submit a one year's worth of salary slips.
  • Provide personal income tax returns for the last 2 years, or an auditor's letter explaining non-filing.
  • Submit company registration copies and three years of income tax returns if owning a business.
  • Provide a personal bank statement for the last 6 months with bank seal and sign.
  • Submit proof of residence (hotel booking details, etc.).
  • Include the confirmed flight tickets (both departure and arrival).
  • Present travel itinerary.
  • Submit a health condition report form.
  • Provide a color copy of an invitation letter and a guarantee letter from South Korea, if traveling for the first time.

South Korea Business Visa (C-3-4):

Requirements:

  • Valid passport for at least 6 months (including any old passports).
  • Two passport-sized photos with a white background (valid if taken within 6 months).
  • Complete the visa application form online
  • Submit a covering letter or a requesting letter for a visa.
  • Provide a recent bank statement for the last 6 months with bank seal and sign.
  • Include company registration copies.
  • Submit the latest 2 years' IT returns or at least 6 months of bank statements with a letter explaining non-filing.
  • Provide occupation or employment proof.
  • Submit one year's salary slips.
  • Include three years of company income tax returns if owning a business.
  • Submit business profile details in the specified format.
  • Provide proof of essential business activities (agreement, purchase order, proof of payment, etc.).
  • Include the confirmed flight tickets.
  • Submit a health condition report form from Korea.
  • Provide an invitation letter from Korea (original, scan copy, or photocopy).
  • Submit a business registration copy of the inviting Korean company (issued within 3 months).
  • Include a guarantee letter from South Korea.

South Korea Short-Term Dependent Visa (F-3):

Requirements:

  • Valid passport for at least 6 months.
  • Completed visa application form.
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs.
  • Family relationship certificate (birth/marriage certificate).
  • Invitation letter from the primary visa holder.
  • Proof of financial stability (bank statements of primary visa holder).
  • Copy of primary visa holder’s valid visa.
  • Criminal background check (if required).
  • Confirmed flight tickets.
  • Health condition report (if required by Korean authorities).

Disclaimer: Visa approval is subject to the discretion of the Embassy / Consulate. Further documentation may be required.
