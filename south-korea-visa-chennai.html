✅ South Korea Visa Services – Frequently Asked Questions

South Korea offers multiple visa categories: Tourist (C-3), Business (C-3-4), Student (D-2), Work (E-series), and Family Visit (F-1/F-3). Each visa type has specific requirements such as invitation letters, financial proof, or admission documents.

Applicants must provide a valid passport, completed visa application form, recent photographs, bank statements, flight & hotel bookings, and covering letter. For business, study, or work visas, additional documents such as invitation letters, admission letters, or employment contracts are mandatory.
