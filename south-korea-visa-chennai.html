Introduction:

South Korea, officially known as the Republic of Korea, is a vibrant and dynamic country located in East Asia. With a rich cultural heritage and a thriving economy, South Korea has made significant advancements in various fields. One of the notable features of South Korea is its technological prowess.
In addition to its modernity, South Korea has preserved its traditional culture and historical landmarks. The country boasts numerous UNESCO World Heritage sites, such as the Changdeokgung Palace and the Jongmyo Shrine, which reflect its rich history and architectural brilliance. About the Visa Process:

The visa process for Indian nationals traveling to South Korea involves a few steps. First, applicants need to complete the visa application form and submit it along with supporting documents such as a valid passport, recent photographs, flight itinerary, hotel booking, and proof of financial stability. The application is then reviewed by the South Korean embassy or consulate, and if approved, a visa is issued. The process typically takes around 10-12 working days, but it may vary depending on the individual's circumstances.

South Korea Tourist Visa Requirements:

  • Application Form Fill Online.
  • Original Passport (If there are old Passports, they also need to be submitted).
  • Two Photographs (Size 35x45 mm, white background, glossy finish, 80% face image).
  • Covering letter from the applicant addressed TO, THE VISA OFFICER, EMBASSY OF SOUTH KOREA CHENNAI.
  • Employment certificate (Indian company) or Bonafide certificate of school for student.
  • One Year Salary Slip.
  • Personal Income tax for 2 years, or Auditor letter with Reason for non-filing IT.
  • In case of Ownership, please Submit 3 Year Company Income Tax Return (for Proprietor please Submit 3 Year Personal ITR).
  • Company Registration Copy.
  • Personal bank statement for 6 Months with bank seal and signature with sufficient funds.
  • Resident proof (hotel booking details etc.).
  • Confirmed flight ticket (both departure and arrival).
  • Travel Itinerary.
  • Health Condition Report form.
  • Colour Copy of invitation letter from Korea & Guarantee letter from South Korea required for Fresh travellers (Not traveled to any country before).
