What Is a Degree Certificate Apostille?

A degree certificate apostille is an official authentication issued by the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA), Government of India. It verifies your academic credentials for legal recognition in countries that are part of the Hague Apostille Convention.

Whether you're pursuing higher education abroad, applying for a professional license, or accepting a foreign job offer, apostilling your degree certificate is a mandatory step. Almiraaj simplifies this process for students and professionals across India—ensuring speed, security, and affordability.

Why Apostille Your Degree Certificate?

A degree certificate apostille is essential when your academic qualification needs to be accepted internationally. It verifies the authenticity of your certificate, allowing foreign universities, employers, and immigration authorities to legally recognize it.

Here are the most common scenarios where apostille is required:

  • ✔️ Admission to postgraduate or research programs abroad
  • ✔️ Applying for a work visa or overseas employment
  • ✔️ Immigration and Permanent Residency (PR) applications
  • ✔️ Credential evaluation for WES Canada, ECE USA, or UK NARIC
  • ✔️ Obtaining professional licenses or registrations overseas
  • ✔️ Education verification for countries like Germany, Netherlands, and other EU nations

How to Apostille a Degree Certificate in India

The apostille process for degree certificates involves official validation by Indian government authorities. Almiraaj streamlines this journey—making it simple, secure, and hassle-free for students and professionals.

  1. Document Review: We verify your original degree certificate and supporting academic records.
  2. State HRD Authentication (if applicable): Some universities require prior verification by the State Education Department or HRD Ministry.
  3. MEA Apostille Stamp: The Ministry of External Affairs affixes an Apostille sticker, validating the document for use in Hague Convention countries.
  4. Return & Tracking: We deliver your apostilled certificate via courier or in person, with live tracking and dedicated support throughout.

Processing Time: Typically 3 to 10 working days, depending on the issuing university and state board.
Service Availability: Apostille services are available pan-India, with fast-track support in Chennai, Trichy, Madurai, and Tuticorin.
