What Is a Degree Certificate Apostille?

A degree certificate apostille is an official attestation issued by the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA), Government of India, that validates your academic qualification for legal use in any member country of the Hague Apostille Convention.

Why Apostille Your Degree Certificate?

A degree certificate apostille is essential when your educational qualification needs to be accepted abroad. Apostille confirms the authenticity of your certificate so foreign authorities, universities, or employers can legally recognize it.

Here are the most common use cases where degree certificate apostille is mandatory:

  • ✔️ Applying for postgraduate admission or research programs abroad
  • ✔️ Securing a work visa or job offer in a Hague Convention country
  • ✔️ Immigration and Permanent Residency (PR) processes
  • ✔️ Credential evaluation for WES Canada, ECE USA, or UK NARIC
  • ✔️ Applying for professional license/registration overseas
  • ✔️ Verifying education for Germany, Netherlands, and other EU countries

Where Is Apostille Required?

Apostille is recognized by all member nations of the Hague Convention of 1961. If you're planning to study, work, or migrate to any of these countries, your degree certificate must be apostilled by the MEA of India.
