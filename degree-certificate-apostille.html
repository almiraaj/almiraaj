What Is a Degree Certificate Apostille?

A degree certificate apostille is an official attestation issued by the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA), Government of India, that validates your academic qualification for legal use in any member country of the Hague Apostille Convention.

Whether you're planning to pursue higher education abroad, apply for a professional license, or accept a foreign job offer, apostilling your degree is mandatory. Almiraaj helps students and professionals across India get their academic certificates apostilled quickly, securely, and affordably.
