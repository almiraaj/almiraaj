Degree Certificate Apostille

What Is a Degree Certificate Apostille?

A degree certificate apostille is an official attestation issued by the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA), Government of India, that validates your academic qualification for legal use in any member country of the Hague Apostille Convention.

Whether you're planning to pursue higher education abroad, apply for a professional license, or accept a foreign job offer, apostilling your degree is mandatory. Almiraaj helps students and professionals across India get their academic certificates apostilled quickly, securely, and affordably.

Why Apostille Your Degree Certificate?

A degree certificate apostille is essential when your educational qualification needs to be accepted abroad. Apostille confirms the authenticity of your certificate so foreign authorities, universities, or employers can legally recognize it.

Here are the most common use cases where degree certificate apostille is mandatory:

  • ✔️ Applying for postgraduate admission or research programs abroad
  • ✔️ Securing a work visa or job offer in a Hague Convention country
  • ✔️ Immigration and Permanent Residency (PR) processes
  • ✔️ Credential evaluation for WES Canada, ECE USA, or UK NARIC
  • ✔️ Applying for professional license/registration overseas
  • ✔️ Verifying education for Germany, Netherlands, and other EU countries

How to Apostille a Degree Certificate in India

The Apostille process for degree certificates involves validation by Indian government authorities to ensure authenticity. At Almiraaj, we make it simple and stress-free for students and professionals.

  1. Document Review: We check the original degree certificate and supporting academic records.
  2. State HRD Authentication (if required): Some universities may require verification by the State Education Department or HRD Ministry.
  3. MEA Apostille Stamp: The Ministry of External Affairs issues an Apostille sticker validating the document for use in Hague Convention countries.
  4. Return & Tracking: We return your apostilled certificate via courier or in person. You’ll receive live tracking and instant support.

Processing Time: 3 to 10 working days, depending on state and university board.
