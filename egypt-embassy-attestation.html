ATTESTATION FOR EGYPT EMBASSY

Certificate attestation from Home Department, HRD and MEA attestation. The Government of Egypt requires all personal and educational certificates, academic of professional, to be issued from India to be attested by Consulate or Embassy in Egypt. These documents are further needed to be attested by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MEA) of Egypt government before granting visa for Egypt.
1) THE FUNCTIONS AND ROLE OF NOTARY IN EGYPT CERTIFICATE ATTESTATION
Certificates, of all types, have to be attested for Egypt for formal purpose. It all starts with Notary. Once certificates are attested from notary, you are required to submit documents for home department attestation which further states the authenticity of the submitted documents.
2) THE FUNCTION AND OF THE HOME DEPARTMENT IN EGYPT CERTIFICATE ATTESTATION
Before submitting documents to MEA for attestation, you are required to get attestation done from Home Department. Home Department authenticates the certificates after verifying the content of certificates. Authentication is an accurate representation of the authenticity of submitted certificates. All formalities are similar for Egypt certificates attestations. The HRD ministry that is the Human Resource Department of the State attests all certificates issued by Govt. Technical Board, medical as well as paramedical institution, Govt. School, university and other organizations. After completion of the attestation process, certificate is preceded further to the External Affairs & Embassy Attestation. You can also consider Mantralya attestation for get MEA attestation done.
3) MEA ATTESTATION FOR EGYPT
MEA attestation comes under the ministry of external affairs government of India. It is 6the Ministry of External Affairs which attests all the certificates issues by any state in India before making submission to Egypt embassy. Home Department attestation or education department attestation should be done for MEA attestation. Ministry of External Affairs attests all non-educational as well as educational certificates as well as commercial certificates.
4) EGYPT EMBASSY ATTESTATION
The Egypt attestation needs all personal as well as educational certificates, academic or personal, issued from India to be attested by the Consulate or Embassy in Egypt for further attestation by the MOFA which stands for Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Government of Egypt before granting Visa. ATTESTATION FOR EGYPT Once certificates are attested by MEA, you can submit them to the Embassy of Egypt for further attestation. The Egypt embassy will attest all non-educational as well as educational and commercial document if the certificates are attested by MEA.
5) SDM ATTESTATION FOR EGYPT
The office of Sub Divisional Magistrate is located in Delhi.
