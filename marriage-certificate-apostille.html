What is Marriage Certificate Apostille?

A marriage certificate apostille is an official authentication issued by the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA) in India, making your document legally valid in countries that are part of the Hague Convention. This process is essential for NRIs and international couples seeking recognition of their marital status abroad.

To learn more, visit our dedicated page on Marriage Certificate Apostille.

Accepted Countries

The apostille is accepted in over 100 countries including:

  • USA, UK, Canada, Australia
  • Germany, France, Italy, Netherlands
  • Japan, South Korea, New Zealand
  • Spain, Switzerland, Belgium, and more

Why Apostille is Needed

  • Spouse visa applications
  • Family reunification abroad
  • Legal proof of marriage in foreign jurisdictions
  • Employment or immigration documentation

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is an apostille?

An apostille is a certificate that authenticates the origin of a public document for use in Hague Convention countries.

2. Is apostille mandatory for spouse visa?

Yes, most countries require apostilled marriage certificates for spouse visa applications.

3. Can Almiraaj help with urgent apostille?

Yes, we offer expedited services based on document type and location.
