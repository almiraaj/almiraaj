What is Marriage Certificate Apostille?

A marriage certificate apostille is a government-issued authentication that validates your marriage certificate for use in foreign countries. It is essential for legal recognition abroad, especially in countries that are part of the Hague Convention.

Accepted Countries for Apostille

The apostille is accepted in over 100 countries including:

  • Germany
  • France
  • Italy
  • Netherlands
  • Spain
  • USA
  • UK
  • Australia
  • New Zealand
  • South Korea
  • Japan
  • And many more...

Why Do You Need Apostille on a Marriage Certificate?

  • Spouse visa application
  • Family migration or reunification
  • Marital status verification abroad
  • Legal procedures in foreign jurisdictions
  • Employment or residency documentation

Basic Requirements for Apostille

  • Original marriage certificate
  • Passport copies of both spouses
  • Government-issued ID proof
  • Notarization (if required)

Processing Time & Duration

The apostille process typically takes 6 to 8 working days, depending on the issuing authority and document condition.
