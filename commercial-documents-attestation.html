Process of Commercial Documents Apostille / Attestation

Three main departments are involved in the process of commercial document attestation. Two of these departments are consistent across all types of certificate attestation services, while state departments vary based on the required document attestation or apostille in Delhi. The verification process starts with the Chamber of Commerce, followed by re-verification by the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA). Finally, the embassy of the destination country verifies and legalizes the document with an attestation stamp or sign. For apostille commercial documents, the MEA provides the final attestation.

Step-by-Step Process of a Commercial Certificate Attestation Service:

  1. The Chamber of Commerce authenticates the document.
  2. The MEA re-verifies the document and forwards it to the destination country's embassy.
  3. The embassy of the applicant's destination country re-checks the document and legalizes it with an attestation stamp or sign.
