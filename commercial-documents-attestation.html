Commercial Documents Apostille & Embassy Attestation

We provide attestation for a wide range of commercial documents including Certificate of Origin, Certificate of Incorporation, Export Invoices, Packaging Lists, Power of Attorney, and Analysis Reports.

Process of Commercial Documents Apostille / Attestation

The attestation process involves three key departments. It begins with authentication by the Chamber of Commerce, followed by verification from the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA), and finally legalization by the Embassy of the destination country.

  1. Chamber of Commerce authenticates the document.
  2. MEA re-verifies and forwards it to the embassy.
  3. The embassy legalizes the document with an attestation stamp or seal.

For apostille services, MEA provides the final stamp without embassy involvement for Hague Convention countries.
