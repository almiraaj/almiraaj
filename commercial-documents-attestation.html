What is Commercial Documents Attestation?

Commercial Documents Attestation is the process of verifying business-related papers such as invoices, incorporation certificates, MoUs, export documents, and agreements. This ensures they are legally recognized abroad for trade, investment, and corporate operations.

Why is Attestation Important?

Attestation validates your commercial papers for use in foreign jurisdictions. It is crucial for opening overseas branches, securing trade licenses, participating in tenders, and ensuring smooth cross-border transactions.

Apostille vs Attestation

Apostille is used for countries under the Hague Convention and involves a single government stamp. Attestation is a multi-step process required for non-Hague countries involving state, MEA, and embassy verification.

  • Apostille : For Hague Convention countries (e.g., France, Germany)
  • Attestation : For non-Hague countries (e.g., UAE, Saudi Arabia)
