Frequently Asked Questions – Apostille Services in Madurai

An apostille is a certificate issued by the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA) that authenticates the origin of a document for use in countries that are part of the Hague Convention.

Apostille is required when you plan to use Indian documents abroad for purposes like employment, education, marriage, or business in Hague Convention countries.

Documents eligible for apostille include birth certificates, marriage certificates, degree certificates, police clearance certificates (PCC), and commercial documents like invoices and power of attorney.

The apostille process typically takes 3 to 7 working days, depending on the type of document and the issuing state. Expedited services are available for urgent cases.

Apostille is valid only in countries that are members of the Hague Convention. For non-member countries like UAE or Saudi Arabia, embassy attestation is required instead.
