MEA Apostille Process in Madurai

Apostille is a standardized legalization process under the Hague Convention of 1961. For Indian citizens, the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA) issues the Apostille sticker/seal, making documents valid abroad without further embassy attestation.

Apostille is essential for visa processing, overseas employment, higher studies, marriage registration, and immigration in Hague Convention countries. Without Apostille, documents may face rejection or delays.
