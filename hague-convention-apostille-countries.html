Popular Hague Convention Apostille Countries

The Hague Convention of 1961 simplifies the legalization of documents for international use. Countries that are part of this convention accept apostilled certificates without further embassy attestation, making the process faster and globally recognized.

Indians most often require apostille for USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Germany, France, Italy, Netherlands, Spain, and Singapore when applying for higher education, employment, migration, business expansion, or family-related purposes.

Birth Certificates

Required for family visas, dependent visas, and proof of identity abroad.

Marriage Certificates

Needed for spouse visas, family reunification, and legal recognition overseas.

Education Certificates

Degrees, diplomas, and transcripts for higher studies and employment abroad.

Commercial Documents

Business contracts, trade licenses, and corporate papers for global expansion.
