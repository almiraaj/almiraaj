What is Kuwait Visa Stamping

Visa Stamping is a process in which visa is endorsed in the passport. The applicants from India looking to visit Kuwait on a Kuwaiti family visa, work visa is required to get their Kuwait visa stamped from the Kuwaiti Embassy or Kuwaiti Consulate in India.

Why Kuwait Visa Stamping

Kuwait visa stamping is compulsory for individuals who are moving to Kuwait with a permanent visa. Permanent visa is taken generally by individuals to work in Kuwait.Also pcc is the one of the must to enter into kuwait .
