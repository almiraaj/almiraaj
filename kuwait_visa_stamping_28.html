Kuwait Employment Visa Stamping | PCC Attestation

Kuwait Visa Stamping refers to the process of obtaining a visa for entry into Kuwait and having it stamped in your passport. It is a necessary step for individuals who are planning to work, visit, or receive medical treatment in Kuwait. The visa stamping procedure involves submitting the required documents to the Kuwaiti Embassy or Consulate in your home country or through authorized travel agencies. The specific requirements and procedures may vary depending on the type of visa you are applying for and the country you are applying from. It is important to follow the guidelines provided by the Kuwaiti authorities and ensure that you have all the necessary documents and information before applying for a Kuwait visa. We can help you to get Kuwait Employment Visa Stamping and Kuwait PCC Attestation from the Mumbai Kuwait Consulate & Kuwait Embassy New Delhi. We offer both normal and superfast services at very competitive prices. With over 25 years of experience in this industry, we prioritize quality and results over marketing and sales.

Kuwait Employment Visa Stamping | Step by Step Process:
  • Step 1: Obtain a valid entry visa from the Kuwait Ministry, required for stamping at the Kuwait Embassy in New Delhi or Kuwait Consulate in Mumbai.
  • Step 2: Get a Police Clearance Certificate (PCC) issued by the Passport office, with Kuwait Embassy attestation.
  • Step 3: Undergo a medical test at an approved medical center. Prior Gamca Medical Online Appointment is required with a charge of 10 USD.
  • Step 4: Submit the medical report to the Kuwait Embassy. Keep a copy for your records before sending the documents for visa stamping.
  • Step 5: Gather all required documents: Original Passport, Photos as per the Embassy requirements, Visa, PCC, Medical report, Vaccine records, etc.
  • Step 6: Once the visa is stamped, check it and be ready to depart within the specified deadline along with other necessary documents. This completes the Kuwait Employment Visa Stamping process.
