About the Australia Tourist Visa

Introduction:

An Australia Tourist Visa is a type of non-immigrant visa that allows foreign nationals to visit Australia for a short time period. Indian citizens applying for a visitor visa (Subclass 600) can go to Australia for business or tourism-related activities. When applying for a tourist visa, you cannot work for any business or sell services/goods in Australia, as per embassy regulations.

Australia Tourist Visa - Requirements:

  • Original Passport
  • 4 Photos with white background
  • 1419 Visa Application Form (filled & signed)
  • Covering Letter
  • Employment or Business Proof
  • Hotel Confirmation with Itinerary
  • Personal Bank Statement (last 6 months)
  • Income Tax Papers (last 3 years)
  • Pay Slips (last 3 months)
  • Employer's Leave Certificate
  • Children: Leave Certificate from School
  • Relatives: Sponsorship Letter with Passport & Visa Copy

Australia Business Visa Requirements:

  • 2 Coloured Photos with white background
  • Covering Letter on Company Letterhead
  • Invitation Letter from Australia
  • Evidence of Business
  • Personal Bank Statement (last 6 months)
  • Personal and Company Income Tax Papers (last 3 years)
  • Company Bank Statements (last 3 months)
  • Company Registration Copy
  • Pay Slips (last 3 months)
  • Health Insurance (mandatory if above 70 years)

*Note: Visa fees/charges are subject to change. Embassy/Consulate may request additional documents or personal appearance for interviews. Submission of documents does not guarantee visa approval.
