About the Australia Tourist Visa

Introduction:

An Australia Tourist Visa is a type of non-immigrant visa that allows foreign nationals to visit Australia for a short time period. Indian citizens applying for this visa (Subclass 600) can engage in business or tourism activities. However, working or selling goods/services is not permitted during the stay.

Why Visit Australia?

Australia is home to breathtaking natural wonders, cosmopolitan cities, and wildlife unlike anywhere else in the world. From dazzling beaches to the vibrant outback, Australia caters to every traveler's taste. The visa process is simple, and multiple subclasses allow visitors to explore the country for leisure or business, with durations ranging from 3 months to 1 year.

Australia Business Visa - Requirements

  • 2 Colored Photos with white background
  • Detailed Covering Letter on Company Letterhead
  • Invitation Letter from Australia
  • Evidence of Business (e.g., registration, contracts)
  • Personal Bank Statement for the last 6 months
  • Income Tax Papers (personal & company, last 3 years)
  • Company Bank Statement for the last 3 months
