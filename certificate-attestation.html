What is Certificate Attestation?

Certificate attestation is a vital process that ensures the authenticity of documents for use in foreign countries. It is necessary for various purposes, including employment, education, immigration, and business expansion. This verification ensures that documents are legally recognized by authorities abroad.

Types of Documents Attested

Attestation is required for various documents:

  • Educational Certificates: Degree certificates, transcripts, diplomas.
  • Personal Records: Birth certificates, marriage certificates.
  • Commercial Documents: Trade licenses, contracts.

Attestation Process

The process of attestation involves multiple verification steps:

  • Notary Attestation: Initial verification.
  • HRD/Home Department: State-level attestation.
  • Ministry of External Affairs (MEA): National-level verification.
  • Embassy Attestation: Final approval from the relevant embassy.
  • Apostille Attestation: Required for Hague Convention countries.
