What is Saudi Embassy Attestation?

Saudi Embassy Attestation is the official process of verifying and legalizing documents for use in Saudi Arabia. It includes authentication by the issuing authority, state departments, and final endorsement by the Saudi Embassy or Consulate. It is essential for employment, education, family visas, business setup, and residency.

Top 5 Reasons to Get Attestation

  • Employment: Validate educational/professional certificates for job offers.
  • Education: Attest academic documents for higher studies.
  • Family Visa: Legalize marriage/birth certificates for dependents.
  • Business Setup: Authenticate commercial documents for operations.
  • Residency: Required for personal records to obtain residency permits.

Explore the types of attestation services we offer for Saudi Arabia

🔏 Notary Attestation

Verifies the authenticity of documents, signatures, and identities with a notary seal. Prevents fraud and ensures legal recognition.

🏛️ HRD Attestation

State HRD verifies educational documents issued by recognized institutions. Required for studying or working in Saudi Arabia.

📜 SDM Attestation

Sub-Divisional Magistrate attestation is an alternative to State Home Department verification. Adds credibility for international use.

🌐 MEA Attestation

The Ministry of External Affairs validates documents for global use, including employment, education, and migration to Saudi Arabia.

🏠 Home Mantralaya Attestation

State Home Department verifies personal documents like birth and marriage certificates. Required for family visas and legal recognition.

📦 COC Attestation

The Chamber of Commerce attests commercial documents like invoices and certificates of origin. Crucial for international trade and business.

🎓 Educational Certificates

Degrees, diplomas, and transcripts must be attested for jobs, university admissions, and visa processing.

💼 Employment Certificates

Experience letters, offer letters, and salary slips are required to validate your professional background abroad.

👪 Personal Certificates

Birth, marriage, and family-related documents are attested for immigration and visa purposes.

🏢 Commercial Certificates

Company registration, power of attorney, and business agreements for international transactions.

👮 Police Clearance Certificates

Essential for immigration, employment, or residency abroad. Certifies a clean legal record issued by the local authorities.

🩺 Medical Certificates
