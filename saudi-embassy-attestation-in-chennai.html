Understanding Saudi Embassy Attestation:

Saudi embassy attestation is a procedure that verifies the authenticity of foreign documents to ensure their legal acceptance within the Saudi Arabia. It involves the verification of documents by relevant authorities, including the home country's designated departments, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and the Saudi Embassy or Consulate.

Certificates/Documents Can be Apostilled by MEA Delhi-New Updates-2023

The Kingdom of Saudi Arabia is now a member of the Hague Convention of 1965. The Hague Convention abolishes the requirement of further verification from the Embassies after the Apostille stamp. To travel to the country you need to get an Apostille attestation for your personal and professional documents. It is a legalization procedure that helps to show the authenticity and legitimacy of your personal and professional documents before the Saudi Government. An Apostille from one Hague country is acceptable and valid in another Hague member country only.All Indian-originating documents which are going to be used in Saudi Arabia require legal verification in India. People who require an Apostille of documents for Saudi Arabia for particular reasons like higher education, business, employment, etc. need to get Apostille from the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA). The MEA of India is the main authority that handles all the overseas matters of the country and provides the Apostille stamps to the documents.
