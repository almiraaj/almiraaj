History of Albania

Albania has a rich history dating back to ancient times. It was home to several Illyrian tribes and later became part of the Roman and Byzantine Empires. In the Middle Ages, it saw the rise of the Principality of Arbër and the medieval Kingdom of Albania. The country declared its independence in 1912 and has since undergone various political changes. Today, Albania is known for its unique blend of cultures and historical sites.

Visa Process for Indian Nationals

Indian nationals need a visa to visit Albania. The application process is done online and requires submitting documents and attending an appointment. The eVisa is suitable for short stays, including tourism, business, or family visits. Here are the key steps and requirements:

  • Valid Passport: Ensure your Indian passport is valid for at least six months beyond your stay.
  • Application Form: Complete the online application form accurately.
  • Passport Copy: Provide a clear scan of your passport's information pages.
  • Flight Itinerary: Submit a round-trip flight reservation.
  • Accommodation Proof: Provide a reservation confirmation for your stay in Albania.
  • Photograph: Include a recent passport-sized photograph.

The eVisa allows a stay of up to 90 days within a 180-day period. For more details, visit the official Albanian visa website.
