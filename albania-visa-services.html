About Albania Tourist Visa

Planning a visit to Albania? Whether it's exploring beautiful landscapes, immersing in rich history, or enjoying vibrant culture, you'll need an Albania Tourist Visa.

Required Documents for Albania Visa

  • Original Passport (valid for at least 6 months with a minimum of 3 blank pages) + all old passports if any.
  • 2 Scanned Recent Colour Photographs.
  • Visa Application Forms: Completed and signed.
  • Personal Covering Letter: Explaining the purpose of travel.
  • Original Bank Statement: Stamped & updated for the last 3 months with bank seal.
  • Air Tickets: Proof of return flight tickets.
  • Hotel Reservation: Proof of accommodation for your entire stay.
  • Travel Itinerary: Day-wise plan outlining all elements of the trip.

Supporting Documents as per Occupation

If Employed

  • Leave Sanctioned Certificate: With company seal.
  • Salary Slips: Last 3 months.

If Self Employed

  • Business Proof: Registration License/MOA/Partnership deed.
  • Company Bank Statement: Stamped & updated for the last 6 months with bank seal.
  • Company’s Income Tax Returns: Last 3 years.

If Retired

  • Retirement Proof: Pension book, statement, etc.

If Student

  • ID Card: From school, college, or institute.

If Minor

  • Birth Certificate: Showing the names of both parents.
  • Legalized Letter of Consent (NOC).
  • If travelling with one parent, the letter must be legalized by the other parent.
  • If travelling alone or without either parent, a notarized letter from both parents.
  • Death Certificate: If one or both parents are deceased.
  • ID Proof: Of parents.

If Visiting Friend or Relative

  • Invitation Letter: Stating relationship and purpose of the visit.
  • ID Proof of Inviter: Passport or Resident Permit.
  • Address Proof of Inviter: Utility bill.

If Sponsored

  • Sponsorship Letter: Providing visitor details, purpose of visit, relationship, length of stay, and additional details if necessary.
  • ID Proof of Inviter: Passport or Resident Permit.
  • Address Proof of Inviter: Utility bill.
  • Proof of Financial Support: Updated bank statement, pay slips.

Business Visa

  • Introduction/Recommendation Letter: From Chambers of Commerce, Ministry of Commerce, Export Promotion Council, or Foreign Affairs.
  • Registration Certificate: From the Ministry of Commerce.
  • Income Tax Documents: Last 3 years.
  • Covering Letter: From the company introducing the applicant.
  • Invitation Letter: From the local partner in Albania.
  • Local Partner's License: Copy of the license (JAWAZ) from Albania.

Note: The Embassy/Consul General may ask for additional information or documents and request a personal interview with the applicant.

Visa Details

Type: "C" Tourist Short Stay.
Processing Time: Approximately 15 days.
Stay Period: Up to 30 days.
Validity: 6
