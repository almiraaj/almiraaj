Birth Certificate Attestation for UAE - Required documents need to be submitted as follows :

Birth Certificate Attestation for UAE is carried out to obtain family residence visa. Birth Certificate Attestation for UAE requires the following mandatory supporting documents.
  • Original Birth Certificate.
  • Other supportive documents as per the Ministry / Consulate /Embassy requirements.

Birth Certificate Attestation for UAE has the following process. In the beginning, the certificates are attested by the Home Department of the state, where the Birth was registered. After that, MEA attestation is done. Next, there is UAE embassy consulate attestation, culminating in attestation by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MOFA).
