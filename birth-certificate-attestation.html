Birth Certificate Attestation for Saudi Arabia

- Required documents need to be submitted as follows
  • 1,Original Birth Certificate.
  • 2,Other supportive documents as per the Ministry / Consulate / Embassy requirements.

Note : Supporting documents can differ case to case , kindly confirn while submit the documents.
The standard process for Birth certification attestation for Saudi Arabia is described as follows. First, the documents are attested by the State Home Department of the respective state in India. Next, there is MEA attestation and lastly, Saudi embassy consulate attestation takes place on both the original and translated certificates. The time taken usually for Birth Certificate Attestation is approx. ten working days.

Birth Certificate Attestation for UAE - Required documents need to be submitted as follows :

Birth Certificate Attestation for UAE is carried out to obtain family residence visa. Birth Certificate Attestation for UAE requires the following mandatory supporting documents.
  • Original Birth Certificate.
  • Other supportive documents as per the Ministry / Consulate /Embassy requirements.

Birth Certificate Attestation for UAE has the following process. In the beginning, the certificates are attested by the Home Department of the state, where the Birth was registered. After that, MEA attestation is done. Next, there is UAE embassy consulate attestation, culminating in attestation by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MOFA).
