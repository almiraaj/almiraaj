MEA Attestation Services

MEA refers to the Ministry of External Affairs, a government department in charge of international policy. One important phase in the attestation procedure is considered to be the MEA attestation. An important phase in the document attestation process in India is the Ministry of External Affairs attestation, or MEA attestation.The Ministry of External Affairs promotes the government's involvement in foreign affairs. The ministry of external affairs is actually in charge of maintaining appropriate foreign relations. MEA will have a minister selected by the government. It involves the Ministry of External Affairs attesting documents, which is a requirement for many documents to be accepted globally.

Why MEA Attestation

  • The Ministry of External Affairs All original documents/True copies requiring attestation or Apostille should be first authenticated by the designated authorities of the State/Union Territory from where the document has been issued.

      • State HRD Authentication :In case of educational documents, the documents should first be authenticated by the Education Department of the concerned State Government/Union Territory.
