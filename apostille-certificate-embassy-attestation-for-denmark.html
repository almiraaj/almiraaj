About Apostille Certificate For Denmark

What is an apostille certificate for Denmark in India?An Apostille is basically a certification of document issued by a legal or registered authority in a country. If a document has been Apostilled it means that the document is verified and it is legal to use in the country to which it has been Apostilled.In accordance with the Convention, in order for Indian documents to be valid in Denmark, they must bear an apostille certificate confirming the capacity of the government official signing the document, authenticity of the signature and, where appropriate, of the seal or stamp on the document.

Why Apostille for Denmark ?

In international law, Apostille/Attestation is the process of certifying a document so a foreign country’s legal system will recognize it. The process is used routinely in international commerce.
