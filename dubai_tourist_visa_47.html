Important Visa Information

  • The passport must have a validity of more than six months.
  • Visa approval is strictly at the discretion of the Immigration Authority.
  • A visa application may be rejected if there is an existing Resident Visa, unused Tourist Visa, or Work Permit Visa that has not been canceled.
  • Applications without a surname are subject to immigration approval. (Any surname-related issues at the airport will not be our responsibility.)
  • Some applications may be randomly selected for blacklist verification, affecting processing times without a definite timeline commitment.
  • Almiraaj is not responsible for honoring any ticket commitments made by the traveler.
  • Visa fees are non-refundable in the event of a cancellation.
  • Even a one-day overstay will be considered absconding. If an individual has not exited or renewed their visa in time, absconding charges will be applicable to the customer (approximately AED 1100).
