HRD ATTESTATION.

HRD attestation is a legalization process done to authenticate an educational / personal /other document. It is the first step of document legalization in India.HRD attestation well known as the human resource development attestation is the validation done to authenticate the documents.

DETAILS

SDM ATTESTATION.

Sub-Divisional Magistrate Attestation is done for personal and educational documents in the absence of concerned state Home department attestation. ... SDM attestation is done for personal documents like birth certificate, marriage certificate, Death certificate, PCC and for educational documents.

DETAILS

MEA ATTESTATION.

General MEA Attestation. Attestation of documents from Ministry of External Affair (MEA), Government of India, New Delhi, is required to make it valid for use throughout India and in a foreign country. MEA is the only apex body having the authority to attest the documents issued from India.
