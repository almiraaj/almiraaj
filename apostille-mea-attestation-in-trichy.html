What is Apostille Attestation?

Apostille is a form of document authentication accepted in countries that are part of the Hague Convention of 1961. It is issued by the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA), Government of India, and is required for documents intended for use abroad—especially in countries like France, Germany, Italy, Netherlands, and the USA.
