What is an Apostille?

An Apostille is an international certification issued by the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA) that authenticates documents for use in countries that are part of the Hague Convention. It confirms the originality of your document and is recognized in over 90 countries. Apostille is essential for travel, education, employment, and legal purposes abroad.
