HRD Attestation Tamil Nadu

HRD attestation in Chennai, facilitated by the e-Sanad initiative, offers online document verification, attestation, and apostille services. Here’s how it works:
Registration: Visit the e-Sanad Portal and register.
Document Upload: Keep a soft copy of your Birth/Death Certificate (in PDF format) ready for uploading.
Process: Submit your documents online for attestation/apostille.
Verification: The concerned Document Issuing Authority (DIA) verifies the digital copy of your documents.
Attestation/Apostille: Once verified, the Ministry of External Affairs attests or apostilles the documents. The digital copy is then sent to you via speed post.
