what is called hrd attestation ?

HRD attestation in India refers to the Human Resource Development (HRD) Attestation. It is a legal process performed by the concerned State HRD department to verify and prove the authenticity of educational degree certificates. When the degree bears the stamp of the concerned authority from the HRD department, it serves as a stamp of approval by the government to render the document authentic. This process is essential for individuals planning to travel abroad for education or employment, as it ensures that their educational documents are recognized and accepted in the destination country.
